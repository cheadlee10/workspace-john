## REVISED STRATEGY

### Option A: Website Upgrade Pitch (For Existing Sites)

**Target:** Crooks & Kings (has site but missing features)

**Pitch:**
> "Hey [Name], I came across your site — crooksandkingsbarber.com. Looks great! Quick question: Are you using an online booking system? I didn't see one on the site.
>
> Here's why I ask: Most barbers I work with see 30-40% more bookings when they add online scheduling. People want to book at 11pm when they remember, not wait until you're open to call.
>
> I can add a booking system to your site in 2-3 days — integrates with Booksy, Fresha, or Square. Usually pays for itself in the first week.
>
> Got 10 minutes to chat? I'll show you what it looks like."

**Pricing:**
- $300-500 one-time to add booking system + improvements
- OR $100/month to manage it for them

**Close Rate:** Higher (30-40%) because they already VALUE having a website
