I also offer a $500 basic option and a $2,000 premium option with online booking and payments. Want me to walk you through the differences?

### "I don't have time for this right now."
> Totally understand — you're busy. That's actually why most of my clients hire me. I handle everything. You just send me your logo, some photos, and your services. I build it, show it to you, and you're live. Takes about 30 minutes of your time total. Still interested in a quick call?

### "I'm happy with Facebook."
> Facebook is great for people who already know you. But 85% of new customers search Google first — and Facebook pages don't show up in those searches. You're losing 8 out of 10 potential customers who've never heard of you. A website captures both — the people who know you AND the people searching Google. Make sense?

### "My nephew/friend said they'd build it."
> That's awesome! If they can deliver something professional and get it done fast, definitely go with them. Just be aware — most business owners I talk to have been waiting 6+ months for that to happen. If you want it done fast and professionally, I'm here. If not, no worries.

### "Let me think about it."
> Of course. What specifically are you thinking about? Cost? Timeline? Features? Let me address that now so you have all the info you need to decide.

### "Send me some info."
> Happy to. What's the best email? I'll send over some examples and pricing, and then let's schedule a 10-minute call for [day] at [time] to go over any questions. Does that work?
