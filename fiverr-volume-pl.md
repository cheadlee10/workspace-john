## Bottleneck Analysis

### What Limits Throughput?
1. **Client communication** (30% of time: questions, clarifications, revisions)
2. **Fiverr platform overhead** (browsing requests, responding to inquiries)
3. **Quality control** (can't just auto-generate and ship without review)
4. **Reputation building** (first 2 weeks = slower as reviews accumulate)
5. **Token budget constraint** (if we cap at $5/day = ~13 jobs max)

### Realistically Sustainable Rate
**Target: 8-12 jobs/day = $130-200/day = $3,900-6,000/month**

This assumes:
- Mix of micro/simple/medium (no complex jobs that eat 2+ hours)
- Efficient client communication (templated responses)
- Batch processing where possible
- Quality spot-checks, not line-by-line review
- 4-6 hours of active work/day

## Risk Factors

1. **Quality at scale:** Can I maintain quality doing 10-15 jobs/day?
2. **Client satisfaction:** Fiverr heavily penalizes bad reviews
3. **Platform saturation:** Lots of $5-20 sellers competing
4. **Burnout:** 10+ jobs/day sustained = brutal pace
5. **Token cost spikes:** Complex jobs or lots of revisions

---

## Strategic Recommendation

### Hybrid Model
**Fiverr:** Volume play (8-12 jobs/day, $130-200/day, $4K-6K/month)  
**Website:** Premium play (1-2 deals/month, $500-2000/deal, $1K-4K/month)

**Combined:** $5K-10K/month net  
**Token cost:** $50-100/month  
**Net margin:** 98%+

### Why This Works
- Fiverr builds reputation + cash flow fast
- Website captures high-ticket clients (no Fiverr 20% fee)
- Portfolio from Fiverr feeds website credibility
- Diverse revenue (not platform-dependent)
