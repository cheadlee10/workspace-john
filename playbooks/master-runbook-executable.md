## 1) Objective
Close and deliver local-business website projects (no-site and outdated-site targets) with predictable weekly output:
- **50 outreach touches/week minimum**
- **5–7 demos/week**
- **2–3 closes/week**
- **One-time revenue:** $500–$2,000/client
- **MRR attach:** $100–$300/month maintenance

## 2) ICP + Offer
## ICP (priority order)
1. Landscaping, contractors, cleaning, barbers/salons, auto repair
2. Businesses with no website, Facebook-only, or outdated site
3. Local radius around Woodinville/Bellevue/Kirkland/Bothell/Redmond/Everett/Marysville

## Core Offer Stack
- **Tier 1 (Basic): $500** — 5-page site, mobile, contact form, maps, SEO basics (3–5 days)
- **Tier 2 (Pro): $1,000** — Tier 1 + gallery/testimonials/booking + analytics (5–7 days)
- **Tier 3 (Premium): $2,000** — Tier 2 + payments/automation/reporting/priority (7–10 days)
- **Maintenance (attach every deal): $100/$200/$300 per month**

## 4) SOP Sequence (Lead → Close → Handoff)
1. Build/refresh lead list (`leads.jsonl`)
2. Run first-touch call/email sequence
3. Qualify in 2–3 min (timeline, need, decision-maker)
4. Book 10-minute demo
5. Present 3-tier offer + recommend Tier 2 anchor
6. Handle objections (script pack)
7. Close and collect payment commitment
8. Handoff brief to Cliff (scope + assets + timeline)
9. Delivery review + revisions (1–2 rounds)
10. Launch + maintenance upsell + testimonial ask

Use detailed SOP: `scripts/sop-lead-to-close.md`
