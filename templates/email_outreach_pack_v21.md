## Conversion Tips (Use Before Hitting Send)
- Keep paragraphs short (1–2 lines).
- Use one clear CTA per email.
- Personalize first line with a real observation.
- Aim for mobile readability.
- Do not include multiple links in early touchpoints.
- Test 2 subject lines per segment and track reply rates weekly.
