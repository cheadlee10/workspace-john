## Fast Personalization Checklist (Before Send)
- Mention one real detail about their business/site/profile.
- Use one concrete pain point only (avoid laundry lists).
- Keep ask small (teardown, 1-page plan, 15-minute check).
- Match tone to niche (professional, direct, practical).
- Send plain-text first; avoid heavy formatting.
