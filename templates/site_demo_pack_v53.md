## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (for sales call or embedded walkthrough)

### Demo Goal
Show a prospect how one messy manual workflow becomes a repeatable, low-touch automation in under 10 minutes.

### Demo Setup (pre-call)
- Prepare a sample “before” spreadsheet or intake form with realistic dummy data
- Prepare “after” outputs: cleaned data tab, status dashboard, and auto-generated summary report/email
- Keep one visible timer or “manual effort estimate” note for contrast

### Step-by-Step Script

**Step 1 — Frame the pain (1 min)**  
“Here’s the exact kind of task teams do every week: collecting inputs, cleaning data, updating status, and sending the same report again and again.”

**Step 2 — Show manual baseline (1–2 min)**  
Quickly walk through manual clicks/copy-paste/formulas.  
Say: “This usually takes about [X] minutes per run and happens [N] times per week.”

**Step 3 — Trigger automation (2 min)**  
Run the automated workflow (button, script trigger, or scheduled run simulation).  
Narrate what’s happening:
- Input validated
- Data standardized
- Exceptions flagged
- Dashboard/report updated
- Notification sent

**Step 4 — Compare outcomes (1–2 min)**  
“Manual: ~[X] minutes/run + inconsistent quality. Automated: ~[Y] minutes of oversight.”  
Highlight concrete gains:
- Time saved
- Error reduction
- Better visibility

**Step 5 — Make it about their workflow (2 min)**  
Use discovery prompts:
- “Which recurring task on your team is most painful right now?”
- “How often does it happen?”
- “What breaks when it’s delayed?”

**Step 6 — Close to next step (1 min)**  
“Next step is a short Automation Audit. We map one bottleneck, define scope, and give you a delivery plan with timeline and fixed price options.”

### Demo CTA Variants
- **Primary:** Book Your Automation Audit
- **Secondary:** Get a Bottleneck Breakdown
- **Low-friction:** Send Me a 2-Minute Demo Video
