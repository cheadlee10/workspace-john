## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy turns a manual, error-prone process into an automated workflow with visible ROI.

### Demo Setup
Use a fictional but realistic scenario: inbound lead form → CRM update → team alert → follow-up task creation → dashboard update.

---

### Talk Track Script

**Step 1 — Problem Framing (30–45 sec)**  
“Most teams don’t have a tool problem—they have a handoff problem. Leads come in, someone copies data manually, follow-ups are delayed, and reporting is always behind.”

**Step 2 — Show Manual Baseline (30 sec)**  
Briefly show the ‘before’: inbox, spreadsheet, and missed status updates.  
“Here’s what this looked like before: multiple manual touchpoints, no ownership clarity, and high error risk.”

**Step 3 — Trigger Event (45 sec)**  
Submit a sample lead form/live trigger.  
“As soon as this form is submitted, the workflow starts instantly.”

**Step 4 — Automation Sequence (60–90 sec)**  
Walk through each automatic action:
1. Lead is added to CRM with clean field formatting
2. Sales owner is assigned by rule
3. Slack/Email alert is sent to the right person
4. Follow-up task with due date is created
5. Dashboard updates pipeline metrics

Narration:  
“No copy-paste, no missed steps, no waiting for someone to remember what happens next.”

**Step 5 — ROI Snapshot (45 sec)**  
“Now multiply this by every lead and every week. This one workflow typically saves [X] hours weekly and improves follow-up consistency immediately.”

**Step 6 — CTA Close (20–30 sec)**  
“We start with a free automation audit, identify your biggest bottleneck, and deliver a practical build your team can use right away.”
