## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (For Calls or Embedded Walkthrough)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy turns one messy manual workflow into a reliable automated process in minutes.

### Demo Length
8–12 minutes

### Script Structure

#### Step 1 — Set the Scenario (1–2 min)
“Let’s use a common example: new leads coming from a web form, then manually copied into a spreadsheet, assigned in CRM, and followed up by email. This is where delays and missed opportunities usually happen.”

#### Step 2 — Show the ‘Before’ Friction (1 min)
- Manual copy/paste between tools
- Inconsistent lead assignment
- Follow-ups depending on memory
- No clear real-time visibility

Narration line:  
“Nothing here is broken in isolation — it’s the handoffs that are costing time and revenue.”

#### Step 3 — Show the Automated Flow (3–4 min)
1. Form submission triggers workflow instantly.
2. Lead is validated and enriched.
3. CRM record is created/updated automatically.
4. Owner is assigned by rules (territory, service, or availability).
5. Follow-up email/SMS task fires with SLA timer.
6. Dashboard updates pipeline and response-time metrics.
7. Alerts trigger if anything fails or stalls.

Narration line:  
“This replaces repetitive coordination with a system that runs the same way every time.”

#### Step 4 — Show Business Impact (1–2 min)
- Faster first response times
- Better lead coverage
- Lower admin load
- Cleaner data for decisions

Narration line:  
“The real win is consistency — your team gets time back, and your pipeline gets more predictable.”

#### Step 5 — Clear Next Step CTA (1 min)
“If this reflects even part of your current workflow, the next step is a short automation audit. We’ll map your process, identify the highest-ROI fix, and give you a practical build plan.”

**Demo CTA Button/Text:** Book My Automation Audit
