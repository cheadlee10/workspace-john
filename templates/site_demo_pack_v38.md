## 4) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 5–7 Minutes)

Use this for live calls, Loom videos, or embedded “How it Works” section.

### Demo Goal
Show how a manual process becomes an automated flow that saves time and reduces errors.

### Script Structure

#### Step 1 — Set the baseline (45–60 sec)
“Here’s the current process: data comes in from forms/email, someone copies it into a sheet, updates status manually, and sends follow-ups by hand. It works—but it’s slow and inconsistent.”

#### Step 2 — Show the trigger (45 sec)
“When a new lead/client request is submitted, the automation instantly captures the data and creates a clean record in your tracking system.”

#### Step 3 — Show routing + task creation (60 sec)
“Based on service type, budget, or urgency, the system routes the lead to the right owner, creates tasks, and sends internal notifications automatically.”

#### Step 4 — Show communication automation (60 sec)
“The lead receives an immediate confirmation and a follow-up sequence. Your team gets reminders only when human action is needed.”

#### Step 5 — Show reporting visibility (60 sec)
“Dashboards update in real-time: pipeline status, response times, conversion trends, and workload by owner—without manual spreadsheet work.”

#### Step 6 — Show exception handling (45 sec)
“If required fields are missing or a step fails, the flow flags the issue and alerts the right person. No silent failures.”

#### Step 7 — Close with ROI (45 sec)
“This exact pattern usually saves [X–Y hours/week], shortens response time, and removes avoidable errors. Next, we map your top bottleneck and build a version tailored to your stack.”
