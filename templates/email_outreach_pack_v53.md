# 5) Deployment Notes (Immediate Use)
- Keep first-touch emails under ~120 words when possible.
- Personalize the first 1–2 lines with a real observation.
- Use one CTA only (avoid multiple asks).
- Follow-up cadence suggestion: Day 2, Day 5, Day 9, Day 14, Day 21 (breakup).
- Track replies by type: Interested / Not now / Not a fit / Referred.
