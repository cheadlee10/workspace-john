## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (5–7 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show a realistic before/after transformation: **manual lead + reporting workflow → automated pipeline with visibility and alerts**.

### Demo Setup (Pre-call)
- Sample web form (Name, Email, Company, Need, Budget)
- Spreadsheet/DB table as source of truth
- CRM board (New → Qualified → Proposal → Won/Lost)
- Slack/Email alert channel
- KPI mini-dashboard (Leads today, response SLA, conversion stage counts)

### Demo Script

#### Step 1 — “Before” (45 sec)
"Here’s the current process: form submissions come in, someone copies data to a sheet, assigns follow-up manually, and reporting gets updated at end of day. This creates delay, inconsistency, and missed leads."

#### Step 2 — Trigger Intake (60 sec)
- Submit a sample lead form live.
- Show instant record creation in the central data table.
- Highlight automatic tagging (source, urgency, service type).

Narration: "Now every inbound lead is captured instantly with standardized fields, no copy/paste required."

#### Step 3 — Auto-Routing + Notification (60 sec)
- Show workflow routing lead to the right owner based on rules.
- Display Slack/email alert with context.

Narration: "The right person gets notified with complete context, so response time drops immediately."

#### Step 4 — Follow-Up Automation (75 sec)
- Show auto-generated first-response draft or sequence trigger.
- Move lead stage in CRM and show synced status update in dashboard.

Narration: "Follow-up starts automatically, and every stage change updates reporting in real time."

#### Step 5 — Live Reporting (60 sec)
- Open KPI dashboard.
- Show lead volume, response SLA status, and stage conversion.

Narration: "Leaders no longer wait for end-of-week reporting. Performance is visible at all times."

#### Step 6 — Reliability + Controls (45 sec)
- Show error-handling path (e.g., missing field → flagged queue).
- Mention audit logs and SOP docs.

Narration: "If data is incomplete, it gets flagged instead of silently breaking your process."

#### Step 7 — Close (30 sec)
"This is the same pattern we apply across onboarding, reporting, and operations: remove manual steps, improve speed, and make outcomes measurable."

**Close CTA:** "If useful, we can map one of your real workflows and show the exact ROI before build."
