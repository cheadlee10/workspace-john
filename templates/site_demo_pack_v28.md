## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual process into an automated system that saves time and reduces errors.

### Demo Setup (Before Call)
- Open a sample “before” spreadsheet/process tracker
- Prepare automation trigger (form submit / new lead / CSV drop)
- Have “after” dashboard or final output view ready

---

### Talk Track Script

**Step 1 — Context (30–45 sec)**  
“Most teams we work with are spending hours each week on repetitive updates, follow-ups, and reporting. Here’s a real example of that manual flow.”

**Step 2 — Show the Manual Pain (45–60 sec)**  
“This process requires copying data, cleaning fields, updating statuses, and sending reminders by hand. It’s slow and easy to miss steps.”

**Step 3 — Trigger the Automation (30 sec)**  
“Now I’ll run the same workflow through the automation. One input triggers the full sequence.”

**Step 4 — Show Automated Outputs (60–90 sec)**  
“You can see records are cleaned, assigned, updated in the right place, and notifications are sent automatically. The dashboard updates in near real time.”

**Step 5 — Business Outcome (45–60 sec)**  
“For teams like yours, this usually means fewer errors, faster turnaround, and 10–30+ hours saved monthly, depending on volume.”

**Step 6 — Close / CTA (20–30 sec)**  
“If this matches one of your bottlenecks, we can map your workflow and propose a first automation sprint this week.”

---

### Discovery Questions (Post-Demo)
- Which recurring task drains the most team time each week?
- Where do handoffs currently break down?
- What systems must stay in your current stack?
- What result matters most in 30 days: speed, accuracy, or conversion?
