## 9) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

> Use this for live demos, Loom walkthroughs, or homepage “How it works” video voiceover.

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy takes a manual, error-prone process and turns it into a reliable automated workflow.

### Demo Scenario
**Use case:** New web lead intake → qualification → CRM update → follow-up task creation → owner notification.

### Script

**Step 1 — Problem Setup (30–45 sec)**
“Here’s the current process most teams have: leads come in from forms or email, someone manually copies details into a spreadsheet or CRM, assigns an owner, and hopes follow-up happens on time. It’s slow, inconsistent, and easy to miss opportunities.”

**Step 2 — Trigger Event (30 sec)**
“I’ll submit a sample lead form now. This is the same trigger your business already gets every day.”

**Step 3 — Automation in Action (60–90 sec)**
“Now watch what happens automatically:
1) Lead data is validated and standardized,
2) A CRM record is created or updated,
3) The lead is tagged by source and priority,
4) A follow-up task is assigned,
5) The account owner gets notified instantly.”

**Step 4 — Visibility Layer (45–60 sec)**
“On the dashboard, you can see new leads, status, owner assignment, and response-time tracking in one place. No manual reconciliation, no hunting across tools.”

**Step 5 — Business Impact (30–45 sec)**
“This is where the value compounds: faster response times, fewer dropped leads, cleaner reporting, and more predictable revenue operations.”

**Step 6 — Close (15–20 sec)**
“If this reflects your current pain points, we can run a free workflow audit and map your highest-impact automation opportunities.”

### Demo CTA (On Screen)
**Book Your Free Workflow Audit**
