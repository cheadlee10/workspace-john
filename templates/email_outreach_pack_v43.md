# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v43

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and appointment setting.

---

## How to Use (Quick)
- Personalize: `{FirstName}`, `{Company}`, `{Industry}`, `{PainPoint}`, `{CurrentTool}`, `{KPI}`
- Keep first touch under 120 words when possible
- Send follow-ups every 2–4 business days
- One clear CTA per email
