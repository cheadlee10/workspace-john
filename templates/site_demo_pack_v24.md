## 8) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 3–5 Minutes)

Use this script for a live walkthrough, Loom video, or sales call.

### Demo Objective
Show how NorthStar transforms a manual process into an automated workflow with visible business impact.

### Demo Scenario
**“Lead Intake to Follow-Up Automation”**

### Script

**Step 1 — Before (Pain State) [30–45 sec]**
“Here’s the current process: a lead form comes in, someone manually copies data to a sheet, sends a follow-up email, and creates a task. It works—but it’s slow and easy to miss steps.”

**Step 2 — Trigger Event [20 sec]**
“I’ll submit a test lead now. This is the only manual action needed.”

**Step 3 — Automation in Motion [60–90 sec]**
“As soon as the form is submitted, the data is validated and added to the master tracker. A follow-up email is sent automatically, and a task is created for the right owner with due dates.”

**Step 4 — Visibility & Control [45–60 sec]**
“Now we can see status in one place: new lead, contacted, scheduled, closed. No copy/paste. No dropped leads.”

**Step 5 — Business Outcome [30–45 sec]**
“This reduces response time, improves consistency, and gives leadership cleaner pipeline visibility. Same team, better throughput.”

**Step 6 — Close [15–20 sec]**
“We build this around your process and tools. If you want, we can map your top bottleneck and show a tailored automation plan.”

### Demo Close CTA
**“Want us to map one workflow from your business and show what automation would look like in your stack?”**
