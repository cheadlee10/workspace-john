# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v30

Ready-to-send outreach assets designed for high reply rates, clear value, and low-friction CTAs.

---

## Quick-Use Notes
- Personalize: **{FirstName} {Company} {PainPoint} {Timeframe}**
- Keep first email under ~120 words when possible
- Primary CTA style: **single-question, low-commitment ask**
- Send from a real person inbox (not a no-reply)

## 8) Fast QA Checklist Before Sending
- Includes recipient first name and company
- Mentions one relevant pain point
- Single clear CTA
- No more than one link (or none on first touch)
- Mobile-friendly formatting (short paragraphs)
