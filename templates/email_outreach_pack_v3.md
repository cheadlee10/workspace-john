## 5) Sending Notes (Immediate Use)
- Keep emails under ~140 words where possible.
- Use one clear CTA only.
- Personalize first 1–2 lines with a real observation.
- Sequence recommendation: Day 1 cold email → Day 3 follow-up 1 → Day 6 follow-up 2 → Day 10 follow-up 3 → Day 14 follow-up 4/5.
- Track reply outcomes and iterate subject lines weekly.
