## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (2–4 Minutes)

### Goal
Show prospects a clear before/after experience in under 4 minutes.

### Demo Setup
Use a sample workflow: **New lead form submission → CRM entry → Slack/email alert → dashboard update**.

### Script
**Step 1 — Context (20 sec):**
"Here’s the manual process most teams run today: form entries are checked manually, copied into a CRM, then someone sends a follow-up and updates a report later."

**Step 2 — Trigger (20 sec):**
"I’ll submit one lead through this form. That single action starts the workflow."

**Step 3 — Automation in Motion (45 sec):**
"The lead is now auto-enriched, tagged by source, assigned to an owner, and a follow-up reminder is created. The team gets an instant alert with next actions."

**Step 4 — Reporting Outcome (30 sec):**
"At the same time, your pipeline dashboard updates automatically—no manual data pull, no delayed visibility."

**Step 5 — ROI Framing (30 sec):**
"For teams running this process daily, this removes repetitive admin work, speeds response time, and prevents lead leakage."

**Step 6 — Close (15 sec):**
"If you want, we can map one of your workflows and show exactly what this would look like in your stack."
