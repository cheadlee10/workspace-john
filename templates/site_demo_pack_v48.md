## 4) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 3–5 Minutes)

### Goal
Show prospects a clear before/after transformation in under 5 minutes.

### Demo Setup (on screen)
- Tab 1: “Before” workflow map (manual steps)
- Tab 2: Automation dashboard (live/placeholder)
- Tab 3: Results summary (time saved + error reduction)

---

### Script

**0:00–0:30 — Hook**  
“Most teams don’t have a people problem—they have a workflow problem. Let me show you a real example of how we remove repetitive work in a few steps.”

**0:30–1:30 — Before State**  
“Here’s the original process: lead comes in, someone copies data manually, follow-up is inconsistent, and reporting happens at the end of the week. This creates delays, errors, and missed revenue.”

**1:30–3:00 — Automation Walkthrough**  
“Now watch the automated flow: lead is captured, routed, tagged, and assigned instantly. A follow-up triggers automatically. Tasks are created for delivery, and status updates populate in the dashboard in real time.”

**3:00–4:00 — Results Snapshot**  
“In this example, we reduced manual effort by **[X hours/week]**, cut response time by **[Y%]**, and improved process consistency across the team.”

**4:00–5:00 — Close + CTA**  
“If your workflow looks similar, we can map your top bottlenecks and give you a clear automation plan. Book a 20-minute workflow call, and we’ll identify your fastest wins first.”
