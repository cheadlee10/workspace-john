## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show prospects a concrete “before vs after” transformation using a common SMB workflow: inbound lead → follow-up → CRM update → reporting.

### Setup (On Screen Before Call)
- Mock lead form (or intake source)
- CRM board/list with sample records
- Messaging/email tool (or simulated notifier)
- KPI dashboard/report page

### Talk Track + Actions

#### Step 1 — Frame the pain (30–45 sec)
**Say:** “Most teams don’t have a lead problem—they have a lead response problem. Let’s show what happens when follow-up is automated.”

#### Step 2 — Submit a new lead (45 sec)
**Action:** Enter a test lead in the form and submit.  
**Say:** “In a manual process, someone has to check inboxes, retype data, and remember to follow up.”

#### Step 3 — Show automation firing (60 sec)
**Action:** Open CRM and show the lead auto-created with owner/tag/status.  
**Action:** Show instant follow-up message triggered (email/SMS placeholder).  
**Say:** “Now the lead is captured, assigned, and contacted in seconds—with no manual hand-off.”

#### Step 4 — Show operational visibility (45 sec)
**Action:** Open dashboard/report and point to updated KPI count.  
**Say:** “Leadership gets live visibility without waiting for weekly spreadsheet cleanups.”

#### Step 5 — Translate to business impact (45 sec)
**Say:** “This is where teams usually recover hours every week and reduce dropped opportunities. The same pattern works for onboarding, reporting, and internal ops.”

#### Step 6 — Close with next step (30 sec)
**Say:** “If you want, we can map this to your exact process and show your top 2 quick-win automations.”
**CTA:** “Book your Automation Review”
