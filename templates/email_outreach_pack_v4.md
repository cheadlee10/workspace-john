## Sending Notes (Internal)
- Keep first touch under 120 words where possible.
- Personalize first line using a real observation (team size, recent growth, hiring, service expansion).
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 5, Day 9, Day 14, Day 21.
- Stop after 5 touches unless prospect engages.
- Track opens/replies and rotate subject lines weekly.
