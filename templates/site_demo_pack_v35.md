## 9) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 3–5 Minutes)

> Use this for sales calls, loom videos, or live homepage demos.

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a common manual process into a reliable automated workflow with measurable impact.

### Setup (30 sec)
“Let’s take one real process: new inbound leads. Most teams manually collect, sort, assign, and follow up. That causes delays and dropped opportunities.”

### Scene 1 — Before State (45 sec)
- Show fragmented tools (form inbox, spreadsheet, CRM)
- Mention typical pain points:
  - Delayed responses
  - Missing lead ownership
  - Inconsistent follow-up
  - Unreliable reporting

Narration: “This is where time leaks and revenue slips.”

### Scene 2 — Automation Build Snapshot (60 sec)
- Show trigger: new lead form submission
- Show actions:
  1. Lead data normalized
  2. Auto-tag by source/type
  3. Owner assigned by rules
  4. Follow-up task + message created
  5. Dashboard updated instantly

Narration: “Now every lead follows the same path automatically, with visibility from first touch to close.”

### Scene 3 — Results View (45 sec)
- Show dashboard with key metrics:
  - Time-to-first-response
  - Follow-up completion rate
  - Stage conversion

Narration: “When the process is standardized and automated, your team spends less time organizing work and more time closing it.”

### Close (30 sec)
“NorthStar starts with one high-impact workflow, proves ROI quickly, then scales automation across your operations.”

**‘Want this mapped to your process? Book a free 20-minute automation audit.’**
