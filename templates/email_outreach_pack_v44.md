## Final Usage Notes
- Keep emails under ~120 words when possible.
- Use one clear CTA per email.
- Personalize the first 1–2 lines for best reply rates.
- Avoid attachments on first touch; offer value in-message.
- Track outcomes by subject line + CTA combo to iterate weekly.
