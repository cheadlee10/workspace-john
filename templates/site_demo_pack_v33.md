## 7) Demo Flow Script (Simple 3–5 Minute Walkthrough)

**Demo Title:** From Manual Chaos to Automated Execution

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy captures a lead, triggers follow-up, updates the pipeline, and produces a live dashboard update—without manual re-entry.

### Demo Setup (Narration)
“Here’s a common workflow most teams run manually: new inquiry comes in, someone logs it, someone follows up, and reporting lags behind. We automated that end-to-end.”

### Step-by-Step Script

**Step 1 — Lead Capture (0:00–0:45)**
- Action: Submit a sample web form (name, email, service request).
- Show: New record appears instantly in CRM/Sheet.
- Narration: “No copy/paste. Intake is standardized from the start.”

**Step 2 — Auto Qualification + Routing (0:45–1:30)**
- Action: Trigger rules evaluate lead type/value.
- Show: Lead is tagged, owner assigned, follow-up task created.
- Narration: “Routing is automatic, so leads don’t sit unassigned.”

**Step 3 — Follow-Up Sequence (1:30–2:20)**
- Action: Prebuilt email/SMS sequence sends first response.
- Show: Message log + next-step reminder generated.
- Narration: “Fast first response improves conversion without adding admin burden.”

**Step 4 — Team Visibility (2:20–3:05)**
- Action: Open dashboard.
- Show: Pipeline counts, response SLA, and conversion indicators auto-refresh.
- Narration: “Leadership gets live visibility without asking for manual status updates.”

**Step 5 — Exception Handling (3:05–3:45)**
- Action: Simulate missing data or high-priority lead.
- Show: Alert sent to team channel + escalation owner assigned.
- Narration: “When something breaks pattern, your team is alerted immediately.”

### Close (3:45–4:15)
“Same team, same tools—just a smarter system. That’s what NorthStar delivers: less manual work, faster execution, and clearer performance.”

**Final CTA:**  
“If you want this mapped to your exact process, book the Automation Blueprint and we’ll design your first rollout plan.”
