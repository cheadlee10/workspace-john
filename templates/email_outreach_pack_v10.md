## How to Use (Quick)
- Replace bracketed fields: `[First Name]`, `[Company]`, `[Pain Point]`, `[Your Name]`, `[Booking Link]`.
- Keep first-touch emails under ~120 words.
- Send follow-ups 2, 4, 7, 10, and 14 days after initial outreach.
- Personalize 1 line minimum in each send (recent post, website note, or offer detail).
