# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v26

**Purpose:** High-converting outbound assets for immediate use  
**Includes:** 5 cold emails, 5 follow-up variants, 3 niche-specific versions  
**Use with placeholders:** `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{city}`, `{sender_name}`, `{sender_title}`, `{phone}`, `{calendar_link}`

---

## Quick Send Rules (Use Before Sending)
- Keep total email length under ~140 words.
- Personalize first line (website, offer, review count, speed issue, etc.).
- Use one CTA per email (reply-based or calendar-based).
- Send follow-up in same thread.
- Remove any placeholder not used.
