## SERVICE-SPECIFIC VARIANTS

### OpenClaw Bot ($500-$5,000)
**Hook:** "What if you had an assistant that worked 24/7, never made mistakes, and cost a fraction of a part-timer? That's what an OpenClaw bot does."

**Use case examples:**
- Discord community moderation + onboarding
- Telegram notifications for business events
- Automated customer intake and routing
- Data monitoring and alerts

### Custom Integrations ($299-$2,000)
**Hook:** "Your tools don't talk to each other, so you're the middleware. I can connect {{Tool1}} to {{Tool2}} so data flows automatically."

**Common integrations:**
- Stripe → Google Sheets
- Typeform → CRM
- Google Calendar → SMS reminders
- Inventory systems → E-commerce platforms

### Reporting Pipelines ($500-$2K + $200-500/mo)
**Hook:** "What if every Monday morning, your dashboard was already updated with last week's numbers? That's what a reporting pipeline does."

**Deliverables:**
- Automated data pulls
- Scheduled report generation
- Executive dashboards
- Alerts for anomalies

### Database Build ($500-$3,000)
**Hook:** "You're running your business on spreadsheets that are held together with duct tape. Let me build you a real database that scales."

**Benefits:**
- Data integrity (no more formula breaks)
- Multi-user access without version conflicts
- Automated backups
- Scalable as you grow

## DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (for calls)

Use these to scope projects accurately:

1. "Walk me through what happens now -- from start to finish."
2. "How often do you do this? How long does it take?"
3. "What breaks? What's the most frustrating part?"
4. "What would 'perfect' look like?"
5. "Who touches this data besides you?"
6. "What tools are you currently using?"
7. "Is there anything you've tried that didn't work?"

## NOTES

**These are high-ticket sales.** Response rates will be lower, but deal sizes are 10-50x larger. One $2K bot sale = 20 website subscriptions.

**Lead with ROI, not features.** These prospects care about time saved and money recovered, not the technical stack.

**The discovery call is where you win.** First email's only job is to get you 10 minutes. Close the deal on the call.

**Recurring > one-time.** Always frame solutions as ongoing partnerships where possible ($X setup + $Y/month maintenance).
