# Email Sequence 1: Website Sales (Local SMBs)
*Primary revenue stream - $0 down + monthly subscription model*
*Created: 2026-03-05 | Version: 1.0*

---

## Target Audience
Local service businesses: landscapers, plumbers, HVAC, cleaning, handymen, contractors, auto shops, salons, etc.

## When to Use
- Business has no website OR outdated site
- Strong Google/Yelp reviews but poor online presence
- Local search visibility gap

---

## SUBJECT LINE OPTIONS (Test & Rotate)

**Question-Based (Highest Open Rate ~46%)**
- Quick question about {{CompanyName}}
- {{FirstName}}, noticed something about your site?
- Missing calls in {{City}}?
- {{CompanyName}} on Google?

**Personalized (22% Open Boost)**
- Idea for {{CompanyName}}
- {{CompanyName}} + Google visibility
- Quick win for {{CompanyName}}

**Result-Based (Highest Meeting Rate)**
- {{CompanyName}} on page 1 in 60 days?
- 12 new leads/month for {{CompanyName}}?

## NOTES

**DO NOT include pricing in any of these emails.** Pricing comes AFTER they engage. The goal is to start a conversation, not close a deal in email #1.

**Visual proof is mandatory.** Never send without the preview link.

**Soft CTA only.** Questions that invite a reply, not demands for action.
