## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show prospects exactly how a messy manual process becomes a clean automated flow with measurable time savings.

### Demo Setup (Before Call)
- Use a sample “before” workflow (spreadsheet + email handoff + manual status updates).
- Prepare a “live” automation mock: form submission → task creation → notification → dashboard update.
- Have one KPI ready (e.g., “cuts this process from 25 min to 6 min”).

### Demo Script

**Step 1 — Frame the Pain (30 sec)**  
“Most teams we meet are stuck copying data between tools, chasing updates, and fixing avoidable mistakes. That slows everything down.”

**Step 2 — Show the ‘Before’ (45 sec)**  
“Here’s the current process: intake comes in, someone retypes details, another person updates status, and reporting is manual at the end of the week.”

**Step 3 — Show the Automated Flow (90 sec)**  
“Now watch this: a new request is submitted once, data routes automatically, the right person is notified, and your dashboard updates in real time — no copy/paste.”

**Step 4 — Business Impact (45 sec)**  
“This single automation saves about `[X]` hours each week, reduces handoff errors, and gives management instant visibility.”

**Step 5 — Close with Next Step (30 sec)**  
“If this matches one of your bottlenecks, we can map your highest-ROI workflow and deliver a first automation quickly.”

**CTA:** “Would you like us to identify your best first automation in a free workflow audit?”
