## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show a before/after transformation from manual busywork to automated execution with measurable impact.

### Demo Setup (example scenario)
**Scenario:** New web lead submission triggers instant ops workflow.

**Tools shown (example):** Form, spreadsheet/CRM, team chat/email notification, dashboard row update.

---

### Script

**Step 1 — Frame the pain (20–30 sec)**
“Here’s the typical problem: a lead comes in, someone copies details into multiple tools, follow-up is delayed, and reporting gets updated later—if at all.”

**Step 2 — Show manual baseline (30–45 sec)**
- Open source form inbox or lead feed
- Show duplicate entry points (sheet + CRM + message)
- Call out delay/error risk

**Step 3 — Trigger the automation live (60 sec)**
- Submit a sample lead
- Narrate: “Watch what happens in the next 5–10 seconds.”

**Step 4 — Show system actions (60–90 sec)**
- Lead record created/updated automatically
- Owner assignment/routing applied
- Instant follow-up message queued/sent
- Team alert posted with context
- KPI/dashboard counter updated

**Step 5 — Quantify outcome (30–45 sec)**
“Instead of 6–8 manual touches and delayed response, this runs instantly and consistently. That usually means faster response times, fewer dropped leads, and cleaner reporting.”

**Step 6 — Close with offer (20–30 sec)**
“We can map one of your current workflows in a quick audit and show exactly where automation creates immediate ROI.”

**CTA:** Book a Workflow Audit
