## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (2–4 Minutes)

### Goal
Show prospects how a common manual workflow becomes automated in real time.

### Demo Narrative
**Opening (15–20 sec):**
“Most teams handle this process manually—copying info between tools, sending follow-up emails, and updating trackers. Here’s what that looks like when it runs automatically.”

**Step 1 — Trigger (20–30 sec):**
- Submit a sample form (new lead/request/order).
- Explain: “This is the only manual step your team takes.”

**Step 2 — Automation in Motion (60–90 sec):**
- Record appears in CRM/Sheet automatically.
- Task is created in project board with owner + due date.
- Confirmation email/text sends instantly.
- Internal Slack/Discord alert posts to team channel.

Narration:
“Instead of 5–7 manual actions, one trigger now handles routing, communication, and tracking automatically.”

**Step 3 — Visibility & Control (30–45 sec):**
- Show dashboard/status board update.
- Show audit log/timestamps.

Narration:
“You still keep control. Every action is visible, traceable, and easy to adjust.”

**Step 4 — Business Outcome (20–30 sec):**
- Summarize time saved and error reduction.
- Tie result to revenue or service speed.

Narration:
“This kind of workflow typically saves [X] hours per week and improves response speed immediately.”

**Close + CTA (15–20 sec):**
“If you want, we’ll map one process in your business and show you exactly where automation creates the biggest ROI first.”

**CTA Button:** Book My Workflow Audit
