## Deployment Notes (Immediate Use)
1. Personalize first line with one real observation about the lead.
2. Keep first send under 120 words for cold outreach.
3. Send follow-ups on days 2, 4, 7, 12, and 20.
4. Use one CTA per email.
5. Track replies by type: interested / not now / no fit / referral.
