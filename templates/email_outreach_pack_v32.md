## Fast Usage Notes (Internal)
- Keep emails 70–140 words when possible.
- Personalize first line with one specific observation.
- Use one clear CTA only (reply or book).
- Send follow-ups every 2–4 business days.
- Stop after Follow-Up 5 unless prospect engages.
