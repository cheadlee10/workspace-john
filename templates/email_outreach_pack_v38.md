## Sending Best Practices (Fast)
- Keep first email under ~140 words when possible.
- One clear CTA per email.
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 5, Day 9, Day 14, Day 21 (breakup).
- Personalize first 1–2 lines with a real observation.
- Stop after breakup unless prospect re-engages.
