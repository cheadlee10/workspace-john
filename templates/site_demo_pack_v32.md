## 8) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

Use this as a live screen-share script or recorded walkthrough.

### Demo Goal
Show a clear before/after of one manual workflow transformed into an automated flow.

### Setup (Before Demo)
- Prepare one sample workflow (e.g., lead intake or weekly report generation)
- Have a “manual version” screenshot ready
- Have automation flow and final dashboard/report ready

### Script

**0:00–0:30 — Problem Framing**  
“Here’s the manual process most teams run today: form entry, spreadsheet updates, reminders, and status checks done by hand.”

**0:30–1:15 — Show the Bottleneck**  
“Each step adds delay and error risk. One missed follow-up or bad copy/paste creates downstream issues.”

**1:15–2:30 — Show the Automation Live**  
- Submit a sample lead/form/input
- Show auto-routing/tagging
- Show task/notification creation
- Show CRM/dashboard update

Narration: “One trigger now handles the full chain automatically, with visibility and fallback alerts.”

**2:30–3:30 — Show the Outcome**  
“Instead of 8–12 manual touches, your team reviews exceptions only. That’s where the time savings and reliability gains come from.”

**3:30–4:15 — Business Impact**  
“Typical impact: faster response times, fewer dropped tasks, cleaner reporting, and reclaimed team hours each week.”

**4:15–5:00 — CTA**  
“If you want, we can map one of your workflows and show you exactly what automation would look like in your environment.”

- **Book a Workflow Review**
- **Request a Custom Demo**
