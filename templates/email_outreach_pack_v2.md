# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v2

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation, appointment setting, and service sales.

---

## Quick Personalization Framework (Use Before Sending)
- **{first_name}** = prospect’s first name
- **{company}** = business name
- **{niche_signal}** = one specific observation (Google reviews, response time, ad creative, listing quality, checkout flow, etc.)
- **{pain_point}** = likely bottleneck (leads, response speed, no-shows, conversion, admin drag)
- **{offer}** = your core service (automation, outreach systems, workflow optimization, lead handling)
- **{cta_link}** = calendar link

Keep each email under ~130 words where possible.

## Usage Notes (Immediate Execution)
1. Send Cold Email #3 (“Should I send the audit notes?”) to coldest leads for highest reply rate.
2. Send 3 follow-ups minimum before marking a lead unresponsive.
3. Personalize one line only ({niche_signal}) to scale volume without killing speed.
4. Rotate subject lines weekly to protect deliverability.
5. Track outcomes by template in leads.jsonl notes (e.g., `template:C3, fu:F2`).
