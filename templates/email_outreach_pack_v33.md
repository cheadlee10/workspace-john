## Fast Send Checklist (Before Hitting Send)
- Personalize first line with a real observation
- Keep length under ~140 words when possible
- Use one clear CTA only
- Send follow-up sequence if no reply (2, 5, 9, 14 days)
- Track opens/replies/booked calls in lead sheet
