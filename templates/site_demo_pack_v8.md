## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3-Minute Walkthrough)

### Demo Goal
Show a manual lead workflow transformed into an automated system with visible, quantified impact.

### Demo Assets (Use as Slide/File Names)
- `01_before_pipeline.csv` — raw lead export with duplicates + missing owner
- `02_workflow_map.png` — trigger/action map of automation
- `03_after_pipeline.png` — routed leads with owner, stage, SLA timer
- `04_kpi_snapshot.png` — response time and conversion trend comparison

### Talk Track (Timestamped)
**0:00–0:20 | Set the context**  
“Most teams don’t have a tool problem—they have a handoff problem between tools.”

**0:20–0:50 | Show the current state**  
“Here’s the manual process: duplicates, delayed assignment, and inconsistent follow-up.”

**0:50–1:30 | Trigger automation live**  
“One trigger standardizes fields, removes duplicates, routes ownership, and creates follow-up tasks instantly.”

**1:30–2:10 | Show the new state**  
“Now every lead has an owner, stage, SLA timer, and next action—without manual reconciliation.”

**2:10–2:40 | Quantify impact**  
“This reduces admin hours, improves response time, and protects revenue by preventing dropped leads.”

**2:40–3:00 | Close with CTA**  
“We can map your highest-ROI workflow and launch quickly. Let’s start with a free workflow audit.”

### Objection Handling (Quick Lines)
- **“We already use a CRM.”** Great—this improves process consistency around your CRM.
- **“Will setup be disruptive?”** We start with one fixed-scope workflow to minimize risk.
- **“How fast do we see value?”** Most teams see measurable gains in the first launch cycle.
