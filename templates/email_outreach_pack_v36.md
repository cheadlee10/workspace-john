# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v36

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and appointment setting.

---

## How to Use (Quick)
- Personalize: **{FirstName}, {Company}, {PainPoint}, {Tool/Process}, {YourName}, {CalendarLink}**
- Keep first touch to 80–140 words.
- Use one clear CTA per email.
- Send follow-ups 2–4 business days apart.
