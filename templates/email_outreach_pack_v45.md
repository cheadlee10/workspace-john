# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v45

**Purpose:** Ready-to-send outbound assets for lead generation.
**Includes:** 5 cold email templates, 5 follow-up variants, and 3 niche-specific versions.
**How to use:** Personalize bracket fields, keep under 120 words where possible, send in 3–5 touch sequence.
