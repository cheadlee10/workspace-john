# Local Service Website Page Blueprint (Reusable)

Use this structure for Home and major Service pages.

## Section 1 — Hero (Above the Fold)
- Headline: **Outcome + Service + Location**
- Subheadline: proof + speed + guarantee
- Primary CTA: Call / Book / Get Quote
- Secondary CTA: View Work / Read Reviews
- Include: trust badges, rating snippet, service area mention

## Section 2 — Proof Strip
- Star rating + review count
- Years in business
- Licensed/insured/certified badges
- Local service area list (top cities)

## Section 3 — Services + Outcomes
- 3–6 service cards
- Each card includes: problem solved, deliverable, estimated timeline, CTA

## Section 4 — Why Choose Us
- 3–5 differentiators
- Must include a concrete reason (response SLA, warranty, fixed pricing, etc.)

## Section 5 — Process (3–5 Steps)
1. Request / Call
2. On-site or virtual estimate
3. Work execution
4. Quality review
5. Follow-up / maintenance

## Section 6 — Results / Portfolio
- Before/after images
- Brief case snapshots with measurable impact
- Optional: neighborhood-specific examples

## Section 7 — Offer + Risk Reversal
- Guarantee statement
- Clear scope boundaries
- Offer expiration (if applicable)

## Section 8 — FAQ (Objection Handling)
Cover at least:
- Pricing model
- Availability/scheduling
- Service area
- Materials/tools
- Warranty and revisions

## Section 9 — Final CTA + Contact Hub
- Repeat primary CTA
- Phone, form, and map links
- Business hours
- Emergency/service urgency routing if relevant

---

## Copy & UX Rules
- Grade 6–8 readability
- Avoid generic claims; use specifics
- One primary conversion action per screenful
- Include CTA after major proof blocks
- Keep form fields minimal (name, phone/email, service, location, details)

## SEO Rules per Page
- Unique H1 + title + meta description
- Internal links to relevant service pages
- LocalBusiness/Service schema where appropriate
- Embedded NAP consistency (name, address, phone)
