## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual process into an automated, trackable workflow in minutes.

### Demo Setup (before call)
- Prepare a sample “incoming leads” form or CSV.
- Have a simple CRM/table view ready.
- Prepare an email/notification template.
- Open a dashboard or spreadsheet that updates automatically.

### Talk Track + Steps

#### Step 1 — Start with the Pain (30 sec)
“Most teams lose time in the same places: copying data, chasing updates, and following up manually. We’ll show how we remove that in one workflow.”

#### Step 2 — Trigger the Workflow (45 sec)
- Submit a sample lead form (or drop a row into a sheet).
- Narrate: “This is the only manual step your team takes.”

#### Step 3 — Show Automated Actions (60–90 sec)
- Lead is auto-tagged by source/service.
- Record is pushed into CRM/project tracker.
- Follow-up email/text draft is generated/sent.
- Internal notification sent to owner/channel.

Narrate outcomes, not features: “No copy/paste. No missed handoff. No delay.”

#### Step 4 — Show Visibility Layer (45 sec)
- Open dashboard showing new record and status.
- Highlight KPIs: response time, lead status, workload queue.

#### Step 5 — Show Control & Safety (30 sec)
- Mention approval gates, fallback alerts, and audit logs.
- “Automation handles repeat work; your team stays in control of decisions.”

#### Step 6 — Close with ROI (30 sec)
“Even this one workflow typically saves [X] hours/week. Most clients start with one bottleneck, then expand once results are visible.”

**CTA Close:** “Want us to map your top 3 automation opportunities this week?”
