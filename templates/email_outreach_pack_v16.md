# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v16

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and appointment setting.

---

## Quick Personalization Framework (Use Before Sending)
- **[First Name]**
- **[Company Name]**
- **[Specific Observation]** (recent post, listing, product page, review trend, etc.)
- **[Pain Point]** (slow follow-up, missed leads, manual admin, inconsistent pipeline)
- **[Desired Outcome]** (more booked calls, faster response times, better close rates)

Tip: Keep each email under 140 words whenever possible.
