# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v8

**Purpose:** Ready-to-send outreach assets to book discovery calls for automation, data, and growth systems services.

---

## Quick Use Guide

- Personalize: `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{pain_point}`, `{metric}`, `{booking_link}`
- Keep first touch under ~120 words
- One clear CTA per email
- Send cadence suggestion: Day 1 (cold), Day 3 (follow-up 1), Day 6 (follow-up 2), Day 10 (follow-up 3), Day 14 (follow-up 4/5)

---

## 5 Cold Email Templates

### 1) Pain-Point + Outcome
**Subject options:**
- `{first_name}, quick idea for {company}`
- `Cutting admin time at {company}`

Hi {first_name},

Noticed {pain_point} at {company} might be slowing your team down.

NorthStar Synergy helps teams automate repetitive workflows (reporting, follow-up, handoffs) so owners get hours back and less slips through the cracks.

If we could reduce that process by even **20–40%**, would a 15-minute walkthrough be worth it?

**CTA:** Reply “yes” and I’ll send 2 practical options for {company}.
