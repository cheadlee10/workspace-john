## Deployment Notes (Immediate Use)
- Send cold email + 2–5 follow-ups over 10–14 days.
- Keep each email under ~140 words when possible.
- Use one clear CTA only.
- Personalize first 1–2 lines for higher reply rates.
- Rotate subject lines to avoid fatigue.

**Recommended sequence:**
1. Cold Email #1 or #2
2. Follow-Up 1 (Day 2)
3. Follow-Up 2 (Day 4)
4. Follow-Up 4 (Day 7)
5. Follow-Up 3 or 5 (Day 10–12)
