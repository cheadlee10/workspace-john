## Response Time Guidelines

| Platform | Response Target | Why |
|----------|-----------------|-----|
| Reddit | <2 hours | Speed wins gigs |
| Twitter | <1 hour | Real-time platform |
| LinkedIn | <24 hours | Professional context |
| Email | <4 hours | Shows professionalism |
| Discord/Slack | <30 min | Chat-based, expect fast |
| Stack Overflow | <4 hours | Community expectations |
| Quora | <24 hours | Less time-sensitive |

**Rule:** Faster = higher conversion. Aim to be first response when possible.

---

## Personalization Checklist

Before sending ANY response:
- [ ] Use their name (if available)
- [ ] Reference specific problem they mentioned
- [ ] Estimate timeline (show you read their request)
- [ ] Provide budget range (transparency wins trust)
- [ ] Include 1 credibility signal (years, clients, example)
- [ ] Clear CTA (DM, reply, schedule call)

**Generic = ignored. Specific = replied.**
