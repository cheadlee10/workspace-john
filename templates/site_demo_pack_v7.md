## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3 Minutes)

### Demo Objective
Show one real-world manual process transformed into an automated flow with visible, measurable impact.

### Demo Assets Checklist
- `before_lead_export.csv` — duplicates, missing owner, inconsistent status
- `automation_run_log.png` — trigger + action log
- `after_pipeline_view.png` — owner, stage, SLA timer, next action
- `kpi_snapshot.png` — response time, throughput, conversion trend

### Time-Stamped Talk Track
**0:00–0:20 | Problem framing**  
“Most teams don’t have a software problem—they have a workflow problem between tools.”

**0:20–0:50 | Show the before state**  
“Here’s the current reality: duplicate records, delayed follow-up, and no consistent ownership.”

**0:50–1:30 | Trigger the automation**  
“With one trigger, we standardize fields, dedupe records, assign owners, and generate follow-up tasks instantly.”

**1:30–2:10 | Show the after state**  
“Now each lead has an owner, stage, SLA timer, and next action—with no manual reconciliation.”

**2:10–2:40 | Quantify impact**  
“This cuts admin workload, improves response speed, and protects revenue from missed follow-up.”

**2:40–3:00 | Close + CTA**  
“We can identify your highest-ROI workflow and launch fast. Let’s start with a free workflow audit.”

### Quick Objection Handling
- **“We already have a CRM.”** Perfect—this improves consistency and adoption around your CRM.
- **“Will implementation be heavy?”** We start with one fixed-scope workflow to minimize risk.
- **“How soon do we see value?”** Most teams see impact in the first launch cycle.
