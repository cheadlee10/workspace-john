# SENDING RULES REMINDER

## Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 9-11am local time (primary), 1-2pm (secondary)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons

## Cadence
| Email | Day | Purpose |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Initial | 0 | Hook + observation + proof + visual/demo |
| Follow-up 1 | 3 | Gentle bump + new stat/insight |
| Follow-up 2 | 10 | New case study/proof |
| Breakup | 17 | Graceful exit |

## Non-Negotiables
- [ ] Under 100 words
- [ ] One specific observation proves research
- [ ] No pricing in first touch
- [ ] Soft CTA only
- [ ] Visual/proof link or demo offer included
- [ ] Plain text — paste through Notepad first
- [ ] No smart quotes (verify no "?" characters)
- [ ] All {{variables}} replaced with real data
