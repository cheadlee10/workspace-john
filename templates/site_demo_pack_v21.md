## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a common manual process into an automated workflow with measurable impact.

### Demo Scenario
**Use case:** Inbound leads from a website form are manually copied into a spreadsheet, assigned by hand, and often followed up late.

### Script Outline

#### Step 1 — Set the baseline pain (30–45 sec)
**Talk track:**
“Here’s the current process: lead comes in, someone copies data, someone else assigns it, then follow-up depends on who remembers. This creates delays, missed opportunities, and inconsistent reporting.”

**On-screen:**
- A static ‘before’ flow diagram
- Spreadsheet with incomplete rows
- Example delayed follow-up

---

#### Step 2 — Trigger the automation (45–60 sec)
**Talk track:**
“Now I’ll submit one lead form. Watch what happens automatically.”

**On-screen actions:**
1. Submit test lead form
2. Lead appears in CRM/Sheet instantly
3. Auto-tagging + owner assignment runs
4. Confirmation email/SMS trigger fires

---

#### Step 3 — Show the operational impact (60–90 sec)
**Talk track:**
“The team no longer copies data or guesses who owns the lead. Every lead is tracked, assigned, and followed up with a consistent SLA.”

**On-screen:**
- Pipeline stage updated automatically
- Task/reminder created for rep
- Dashboard KPI tile increments (new lead, response-time SLA)

---

#### Step 4 — Show exception handling (30–45 sec)
**Talk track:**
“If data is missing or invalid, the workflow flags it and routes it for review—so bad data doesn’t silently break your reporting.”

**On-screen:**
- Validation warning example
- Slack/email alert sample
- ‘Needs Review’ queue view

---

#### Step 5 — Close with ROI and next step (30–45 sec)
**Talk track:**
“This is one workflow. Most teams have 5–10 similar bottlenecks. We prioritize the highest-ROI automation first, launch quickly, then stack additional wins.”

**Close CTA:**
“Book a 20-minute Automation Audit and we’ll map your first high-impact workflow.”
