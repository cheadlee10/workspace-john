## 8) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 minutes)

### Goal
Show a clear before/after transformation using a common ops workflow (lead intake → assignment → follow-up → reporting).

### Setup (before demo)
- Open a sample form/spreadsheet with 5–10 mock leads
- Prepare a CRM or tracking board view
- Prepare an automation dashboard/report snapshot

### Live Script

**Step 1 — "Before" (30–45 sec)**  
“Here’s the manual version: lead enters, someone checks the sheet, assigns it, sends follow-up, and updates status. This is where delays and missed handoffs happen.”

**Step 2 — Trigger (30 sec)**  
“Now we submit one new lead.”  
(Submit sample entry live.)

**Step 3 — Automation in Action (60–90 sec)**  
“Watch what happens automatically: the lead is categorized, assigned to the right owner, follow-up is queued, and the dashboard updates in real time.”

**Step 4 — Exception Handling (45–60 sec)**  
“If data is incomplete or high-priority, the system flags it and alerts the right person instead of silently failing.”

**Step 5 — Outcome / ROI (45–60 sec)**  
“This flow cuts manual touchpoints, speeds response time, and gives leadership instant visibility into pipeline activity.”

### Close (15–30 sec)
“If this is close to your current process, we can map your version in a short audit and prioritize the fastest ROI opportunities first.”
