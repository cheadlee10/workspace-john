## Usage Notes (Fast Execution)
1. Personalize first line with a real observation.
2. Keep body under 120 words when possible.
3. One clear CTA only.
4. Send follow-ups at +2, +5, +9, +14 days.
5. Stop after breakup unless prospect re-engages.
