## 5) Demo Flow Script (Simple, Sales-Friendly)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual intake-to-action process into an automated workflow with visibility and alerts.

### Demo Length
6–8 minutes

### Demo Stack (example)
Web form / inbox → automation logic layer → CRM or spreadsheet → Slack/Discord/email alerts → dashboard summary

### Step-by-Step Script

**Step 1 — Set the scene (45 sec)**
“Here’s the current issue: leads arrive from multiple places, updates are inconsistent, and follow-up timing depends on who’s online. We’re going to fix that with one workflow.”

**Step 2 — Trigger intake (60 sec)**
Submit a sample lead through form/email.

Narration:
“Once submitted, the automation validates required fields, tags lead type, and applies urgency rules.”

**Step 3 — Show routing + assignment (75 sec)**
Display auto-routing into CRM/sheet and assignment to correct owner.

Narration:
“No copy-paste. No inbox hunting. Every lead is routed based on your rules in seconds.”

**Step 4 — Show instant notifications (60 sec)**
Reveal channel/email alert with key details and next action.

Narration:
“The team gets a clean alert with context and owner assignment immediately.”

**Step 5 — Show follow-up automation (75 sec)**
Demonstrate auto-created follow-up task/reminder sequence.

Narration:
“If there’s no response in your time window, the system escalates automatically.”

**Step 6 — Show reporting view (75 sec)**
Open dashboard/report showing volume, response time, and stage movement.

Narration:
“Now leadership gets weekly visibility without manually compiling updates.”

**Step 7 — Close with ROI framing (45 sec)**
“In one workflow, we reduced manual handling, sped up response time, and made outcomes measurable. This is the same model we apply to your highest-friction process first.”

### Demo Close CTA
**“Want this built for your process? Let’s run a 20-minute Automation Audit and scope your first deployment.”**
