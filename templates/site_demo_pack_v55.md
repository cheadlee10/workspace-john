## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 minutes)

### Goal
Show prospects how NorthStar Synergy identifies a bottleneck, automates it, and proves ROI quickly.

### Demo Setup (on-screen assets)
- Mock intake form submission
- Spreadsheet/CRM record update
- Automated notification (email/Slack placeholder)
- Dashboard panel showing before/after cycle time

### Script
**Step 1 — Open with the pain (30 sec)**  
“Most teams we work with lose hours every week to copy/paste work, status chasing, and manual reporting. This demo shows how we remove one of those bottlenecks in minutes.”

**Step 2 — Show manual baseline (30 sec)**  
“Here’s the old process: a form comes in, someone re-enters data into a tracker, pings the team, and updates status manually.”

**Step 3 — Trigger automation (60 sec)**  
“Now we submit the same intake form. Watch what happens: data is routed automatically, records are created/updated, and the right person gets notified instantly.”

**Step 4 — Show visibility + controls (45 sec)**  
“We can track every step, add approvals where needed, and keep humans in control for sensitive actions.”

**Step 5 — Show business impact (45 sec)**  
“Instead of 10–15 manual touches, this flow runs in seconds with fewer mistakes. That’s how clients typically recover 5–20+ hours each week.”

**Step 6 — Close to CTA (30 sec)**  
“We start with one high-friction workflow, prove ROI fast, then scale. If you want, we can map your best first automation in a 20-minute audit.”
