# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v49

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and appointment setting.

---

## Quick Use Notes
- Replace bracketed fields before sending: `[First Name]`, `[Company]`, `[Pain Point]`, `[Your Name]`, `[Calendar Link]`.
- Keep first sends under 120 words.
- Use 1 clear CTA per email.
- Suggested send cadence: Day 1 (cold) → Day 3 → Day 6 → Day 10 → Day 14 → Day 21.

## 5 Cold Email Templates

Would you like me to send that over?

Best,
[Your Name]
NorthStar Synergy
[Phone] | [Website]
