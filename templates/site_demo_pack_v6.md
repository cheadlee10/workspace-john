## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
“Show one messy manual process transformed into a clear, automated workflow with measurable impact.”

### Demo Assets (Prep List)
- `before_lead_export.csv` (duplicates, missing owners, inconsistent stages)
- `automation_run_log.png` (trigger + actions completed)
- `after_pipeline_view.png` (owner, SLA timer, next step)
- `kpi_snapshot.png` (response speed, throughput, conversion trend)

### Time-Stamped Talk Track
**0:00–0:20 | Frame the problem**  
“Most teams don’t have a tool problem—they have a workflow gap between tools.”

**0:20–0:50 | Show the ‘before’**  
“Here’s what that gap causes: duplicates, delayed follow-up, and unclear ownership.”

**0:50–1:30 | Run automation**  
“In one trigger, we standardize fields, dedupe records, assign owners, and create follow-up tasks automatically.”

**1:30–2:10 | Show the ‘after’**  
“Now every lead has owner + stage + next action, and leadership gets instant visibility.”

**2:10–2:40 | Quantify impact**  
“This reduces manual admin time, improves response speed, and protects revenue from follow-up delays.”

**2:40–3:00 | Close + CTA**  
“We can map your highest-ROI workflow and launch quickly. Let’s start with a free workflow audit.”

### Objection Handling (Fast Replies)
- **“We already have a CRM.”** Great—this improves consistency and adoption around it.
- **“Will this be heavy to implement?”** We start with one fixed-scope workflow.
- **“How fast will we see value?”** Typically within the first launch cycle.
