## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3-Minute Walkthrough)

### Demo Promise (Open with this)
“In three minutes, I’ll show how one manual workflow turns into a reliable system that saves time and improves response speed.”

### Demo Assets Checklist
- `before_raw_leads.csv` (duplicates, missing fields, inconsistent statuses)
- `automation_run_view` (trigger log + standardized outputs)
- `after_pipeline_board` (owner assignment, SLA status, next actions)
- `kpi_snapshot` (response time, throughput, conversion trend)

### Timestamped Script
**0:00–0:25 | Context + Stakes**  
“Most teams don’t need more tools—they need cleaner workflow between tools. Here’s a common lead ops bottleneck.”

**0:25–0:55 | Show ‘Before’ State**  
“Right now: duplicate records, missing owners, delayed follow-up, and no clear SLA tracking.”

**0:55–1:35 | Trigger Automation**  
“With one run, the workflow cleans fields, removes duplicates, assigns ownership, and creates follow-up tasks automatically.”

**1:35–2:15 | Show ‘After’ State**  
“Now each lead has an owner, stage, and next action. Leadership gets real-time visibility without manual spreadsheet cleanup.”

**2:15–2:45 | Tie to Business Impact**  
“This is how teams cut admin hours, improve response speed, and protect pipeline revenue.”

**2:45–3:00 | Close**  
“We can map your highest-ROI workflow this week and launch quickly. Let’s run your free workflow audit.”

### Objection Handling (Short)
- **“We already use a CRM.”** Perfect—this improves adoption and consistency around your CRM.
- **“This sounds heavy.”** We start with one workflow and fixed scope.
- **“How soon do we see value?”** Usually in the first workflow, within days.
