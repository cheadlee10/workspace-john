## Usage Notes (Immediate Deployment)
1. Pick 1 cold template + 1 follow-up sequence.
2. Personalize the first 2 lines with a real observation.
3. Keep emails 80–150 words when possible.
4. Send follow-ups every 2–4 business days.
5. Stop sequence after reply or after Follow-Up 5.

**Recommended sequence:** Cold #1 → Follow-Up 1 → Follow-Up 2 → Follow-Up 3 → Follow-Up 4 → Follow-Up 5
