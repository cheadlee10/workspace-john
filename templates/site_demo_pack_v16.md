## 5) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 3–5 Minutes)

### Goal
Show a realistic “before vs after” automation outcome in minutes.

### Demo Setup (On Screen)
- Left: incoming raw data/inbox/task list (messy)
- Middle: automation trigger + logic steps
- Right: clean output (updated CRM/report/task board)

### Talk Track Script
**Step 1 — Context (30 sec):**  
“Here’s the manual process most teams run today: data comes in, someone cleans it, someone else updates systems, then reports are built by hand.”

**Step 2 — Pain (30 sec):**  
“This usually creates delays, duplicate entries, and follow-ups falling through the cracks—especially when volume spikes.”

**Step 3 — Trigger (45 sec):**  
“Now we trigger the NorthStar workflow. The system validates inputs, flags exceptions, and routes clean data to the right destination automatically.”

**Step 4 — Output (60 sec):**  
“Within seconds: CRM fields update, the team gets a task notification, and a live report refreshes with current numbers. No copy-paste, no manual chasing.”

**Step 5 — ROI Close (45 sec):**  
“If this workflow runs daily, you’re saving [X] hours per week and reducing manual error risk significantly. That’s immediate capacity your team can use for growth work.”

**Step 6 — CTA (30 sec):**  
“We can run this same blueprint on one of your live processes and show you exactly where automation creates the fastest win.”
