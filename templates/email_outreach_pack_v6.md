## Recommended Send Cadence
- Day 1: Cold Email
- Day 3: Follow-Up #1
- Day 6: Follow-Up #2
- Day 9: Follow-Up #3
- Day 12: Follow-Up #4
- Day 16: Follow-Up #5 (breakup)

## Usage Notes
- Keep each email under ~140 words when possible.
- Personalize first line with a specific observation about the business.
- Use one CTA per email to reduce friction.
- Track replies by subject line + CTA type and double down on winners.
