## Fast Send Checklist (Before Each Campaign)
- Personalize first line to prove relevance.
- Keep one clear CTA per email.
- Use plain text (no heavy formatting).
- Send follow-ups 2–4 business days apart.
- Stop after 5 touches or explicit no.
