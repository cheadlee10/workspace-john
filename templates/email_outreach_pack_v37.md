## Fast A/B Testing Suggestions
- Test short vs long subject lines (3–5 words vs 7–10 words)
- Rotate CTA style: question CTA vs link CTA
- Personalize first line by trigger (recent hire, new offer, market expansion)
- Use follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 5, Day 9, Day 14, Day 21

## Recommended Sending Notes
- Keep paragraphs to 1–3 lines
- One clear CTA per email
- Plain text style outperforms heavy formatting
- Use real sender identity and signature
