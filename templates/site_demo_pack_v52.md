## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (Sales + Landing Demo)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar identifies a manual bottleneck and converts it into a working automation with clear ROI.

### Demo Length
8–10 minutes

### Demo Script

#### Step 1 — Set Context (60 seconds)
**Say:**  
“Most teams don’t need more tools—they need fewer manual steps. Today I’ll show a before-and-after of a common admin workflow we automated.”

**On screen:**
- A messy spreadsheet or manual checklist screenshot
- A short list: “Current pain: delays, errors, repeated tasks”

#### Step 2 — Show the Before (90 seconds)
**Say:**  
“Here’s the current process: data comes in from multiple sources, gets cleaned manually, then copied into a report template every week.”

**On screen:**
- 4–6 manual steps highlighted
- Time estimate: “~4 hours/week”
- Error risk indicators

#### Step 3 — Explain the Build (2 minutes)
**Say:**  
“We map the workflow, remove redundant steps, and automate transformations and reporting triggers. The team still has control points, but no repetitive data handling.”

**On screen:**
- Simple flow diagram: Input → Transform → Validate → Report → Notify
- Labels: “automated” vs “human approval”

#### Step 4 — Show the After (2 minutes)
**Say:**  
“Now the report is generated automatically, exceptions are flagged, and the team only reviews edge cases.”

**On screen:**
- Final dashboard/report output
- Alert sample
- Time estimate: “~45 min/week”

#### Step 5 — ROI Snapshot (60 seconds)
**Say:**  
“This shift saves roughly 3+ hours each week for one workflow. Multiply that across operations and the impact compounds quickly.”

**On screen:**
- Before: 4h/week
- After: 45m/week
- Savings: 3h15m/week (~13h/month)

#### Step 6 — Close to CTA (30–45 seconds)
**Say:**  
“If you want, we can run this same audit on your process and show exactly where automation gives the fastest return.”

**On screen:**
- CTA button: **Book a Workflow Audit**
- Secondary CTA: **Request a Custom Demo**
