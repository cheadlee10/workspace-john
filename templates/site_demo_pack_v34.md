## 4) Demo Flow Script (Simple, 5–7 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual lead-to-onboarding workflow into an automated, trackable process.

### Setup (Before Call)
- Open a sample lead capture form
- Open CRM with test pipeline
- Open automation scenario dashboard
- Open Slack/email inbox for notifications
- Prepare a simple KPI dashboard showing “before vs after”

### Talk Track + Steps

**Step 1 — Context (45 sec)**
“Here’s the current pain: leads come in, updates are manual, and follow-up is inconsistent. We’ll show this same process fully automated in real time.”

**Step 2 — Trigger Lead Capture (60 sec)**
- Submit test lead via form
- Explain trigger fired: “new lead created”

**Step 3 — Automated Routing + Follow-Up (90 sec)**
- Show lead auto-created in CRM
- Show owner auto-assigned by rules
- Show instant email/SMS follow-up draft sent
- Show internal alert in Slack/Teams

**Step 4 — Ops Handoff (90 sec)**
- Move deal stage in CRM
- Show onboarding checklist auto-generated
- Show kickoff task + calendar prompt created

**Step 5 — Reporting Visibility (60 sec)**
- Open dashboard
- Show lead status, response time, and conversion tracking updated automatically

**Step 6 — Business Impact Close (45 sec)**
“This workflow removes repetitive admin, speeds first response, and gives leadership clear visibility—without requiring your team to learn a new system.”

### Final CTA
- “Want this mapped to your exact process? We can run a 20-minute audit and identify your highest-ROI pilot.”
