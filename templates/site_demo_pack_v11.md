## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (6 Minutes)

> Goal: Show a prospect one automation flow that directly improves speed, consistency, and visibility.

### Demo Timeline
1. **Pain Setup** (0:45)
2. **Before State Walkthrough** (0:45)
3. **Live Trigger Event** (0:30)
4. **Automated Sequence in Motion** (1:45)
5. **Dashboard & KPI Visibility** (1:00)
6. **ROI Framing + Next Step** (1:15)

---

### Presenter Script (Word-for-Word Ready)

**1) Pain Setup (0:45)**
“Most teams we work with lose momentum in three places: lead response delays, manual internal handoffs, and stale reporting. I’ll show you one flow that removes all three.”

**2) Before State (0:45)**
“Before automation, a lead comes in, someone manually copies data into your CRM or sheet, another person sends follow-up later, and reporting gets patched together at the end of the week. That lag costs revenue.”

**3) Trigger Event (0:30)**
“I’ll submit a test lead right now—this is the same trigger your real inbound traffic would create.”

**4) Automated Sequence (1:45)**
“Within seconds, the system creates or updates the contact record, tags lead source, sends personalized SMS/email follow-up, creates an owner task, and notifies the team in your ops channel. No manual copy/paste, no waiting.”

**5) Visibility Layer (1:00)**
“Now in the dashboard, you can see the lead appear instantly with source, stage, owner, and response status. Leadership gets clean reporting automatically—without chasing updates.”

**6) ROI + Close (1:15)**
“This is where clients typically recover 5–20+ hours per week and improve speed-to-lead. If this maps to your bottlenecks, our next step is a focused automation audit and a phased implementation plan.”

**Close CTA (final line):**
“Want us to map your highest-ROI workflow this week?”
