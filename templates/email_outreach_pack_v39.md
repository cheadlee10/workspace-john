Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and client acquisition.

## How to Use This Pack
- Personalize the **[bracketed fields]** before sending.
- Keep first touch concise; focus on one pain point and one clear outcome.
- Send follow-ups 2–4 business days apart.
- Stop after follow-up #5 unless the lead re-engages.
