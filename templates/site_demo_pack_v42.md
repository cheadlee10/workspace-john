## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show a prospect how NorthStar Synergy turns a manual process into an automated, trackable workflow that saves time and improves follow-up.

### Scenario
**Use case:** New inbound lead from a web form.

### Flow Script

#### Step 1 — Trigger (0:00–0:30)
“Let’s start with a new lead submission. The moment a form is submitted, our automation kicks off — no manual copy/paste.”

**On screen:** Form submission → workflow trigger event.

#### Step 2 — Data Routing (0:30–1:15)
“Lead data is validated, tagged by source/service type, and pushed into your CRM and tracking sheet instantly.”

**On screen:** Lead appears in CRM with tags; spreadsheet/dashboard row auto-populates.

#### Step 3 — Instant Follow-Up (1:15–2:00)
“Within seconds, the lead gets a personalized confirmation email and your team gets an internal notification with next actions.”

**On screen:** Outbound email log + Slack/Teams/Email internal alert.

#### Step 4 — Task Creation & SLA Guardrails (2:00–2:45)
“We automatically create an owner task with due dates and reminders. If there’s no response in your SLA window, escalation rules fire.”

**On screen:** Task board item + reminder/escalation path.

#### Step 5 — Visibility & Reporting (2:45–3:30)
“Leadership can see response times, pipeline movement, and conversion trends without anyone compiling reports manually.”

**On screen:** Dashboard with response-time KPI, stage conversion, and source performance.

### Demo Close (Talk Track)
“This is one workflow, but the same framework applies to onboarding, fulfillment, invoicing, and reporting. We start with your highest-friction process and deliver measurable gains fast.”

**Close CTA:**
“If useful, we can run a free workflow audit and map your top 3 automation wins this week.”

## 7) Quick Styling Notes (for the page builder)
- Tone: clear, practical, outcomes-first
- Section spacing: generous (80–120px desktop)
- Buttons: high-contrast primary color, single main CTA per section
- Icons: simple line icons for each service card
- Social proof: keep above the fold or immediately after services
- Keep paragraphs short (1–3 lines max)
