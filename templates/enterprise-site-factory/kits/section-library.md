# Section Variant Library

Use modular sections; every page is assembled from approved variants.

## A) Home Page Sections (recommended order)
1. **Hero** (choose 1)
   - H1 + value prop + dual CTA + trust badges
   - H1 + booking widget + review stars
2. **Core Services Grid** (3–6 cards)
3. **Social Proof Block**
   - logo strip + ratings
   - testimonial carousel + video quote
4. **Process Section** (3-step or 5-step)
5. **Featured Project / Before-After**
6. **Service Area Coverage** (map + city chips)
7. **Offer Banner** (seasonal)
8. **FAQ Accordion**
9. **Final CTA + Contact Form**

## B) Service Page Blueprint
1. Service-specific hero
2. Problem/Symptom explainer
3. Solution + inclusions list
4. Pricing range or estimate framework
5. Why-us differentiators
6. Case study highlight
7. FAQs (service-specific)
8. Conversion block (call/form/scheduler)

## C) City/Location Page Blueprint
1. Localized hero with city name
2. Service relevance for city conditions
3. Recent local jobs/gallery
4. Local testimonials
5. Neighborhood coverage list
6. CTA with local phone tracking number

## D) About Page Blueprint
1. Brand story and mission
2. Team profiles with photos
3. Certifications/licenses
4. Process and standards
5. Community involvement
6. CTA

## E) Trust/Conversion Section Variants

### Testimonials (pick 1-2)
- Grid of short quotes with star rating
- Video testimonial cards
- Long-form case result with metrics

### Guarantees
- Satisfaction guarantee panel
- Warranty matrix (parts/labor)
- Response time SLA block

### CTA Blocks
- Simple: headline + button
- Qualified: headline + 5-field form
- Urgency: limited-time offer + countdown (use sparingly)

## F) Section Copy Rules
- Headline: outcome-first, <= 12 words
- Subhead: clarify audience + area + timeline
- Body: 1 idea per paragraph
- Bullets: benefit-driven, verb-first
- CTA text: specific action ("Get My Roof Estimate")
