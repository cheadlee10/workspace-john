# Image Requirements & Asset Standards

## 1) Minimum Asset Pack per Site

### Mandatory
- 1 branded hero photo (landscape, authentic team/job)
- 6 service photos (at least 2 per key service)
- 4 before/after pairs
- 3 team photos (portrait + in-field)
- 2 vehicle/equipment branding photos
- 1 owner/headshot
- 1 local landmark/service-area contextual image

### Optional Premium
- 30–60s b-roll clips for hero/video testimonials
- Drone stills where legal and relevant

## 2) Technical Specs
- Source resolution: >= 2400px wide
- Delivery formats: JPG/WebP (photos), PNG/SVG (logos/icons)
- Compression target: <= 250KB standard images, <= 500KB hero
- Aspect ratios required:
  - Hero: 16:9 and 4:5 variants
  - Card thumbs: 4:3
  - Team portraits: 3:4
  - Before/after: matched dimensions

## 3) Style Rules
- Real local jobs preferred over stock
- Natural lighting; avoid over-filtering
- Include crew uniforms/branding when possible
- Show outcome/result clearly (not just process)
- Avoid cluttered backgrounds

## 4) File Naming Convention
`{niche}_{city}_{service}_{subject}_{sequence}.webp`

Example:
`roofing_tacoma_replacement_before_01.webp`

## 5) Rights & Compliance
- Client confirms ownership/licensing for all assets
- Signed media release required for identifiable individuals
- No copyrighted logos (unless authorized)
- Track source and approval in asset manifest

## 6) Image QA Gate
Pass only if:
- No blurry/compressed artifacts visible on desktop/mobile
- Alt text present and descriptive for every non-decorative image
- Lazy loading enabled below fold
- Hero image has responsive srcset and next-gen format
