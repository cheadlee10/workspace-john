## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (5–7 minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show a prospect how one manual process becomes an end-to-end automated workflow that saves time and improves follow-through.

---

### Demo Setup (before call)
- Pick one common use case (lead intake, client onboarding, or weekly reporting)
- Prepare a simple “before vs after” visual
- Have one live trigger ready (form submission or status update)

---

### Script (Talk Track + Actions)

#### Step 1 — Context (45 sec)
**Say:**
“Most teams lose time in handoffs: data gets entered twice, tasks are missed, and follow-ups slip. Let me show you a simple workflow we automate in under a week.”

**Show:**
A basic process map with manual steps highlighted in red.

---

#### Step 2 — Trigger Event (60 sec)
**Say:**
“This starts when a new lead/client request comes in.”

**Do:**
Submit sample intake form (or update sample spreadsheet row).

**Show:**
Incoming record appears in source system.

---

#### Step 3 — Automation in Motion (90 sec)
**Say:**
“Now the workflow runs automatically: data is cleaned, tagged, assigned, and the right people are notified instantly.”

**Show:**
- Record pushed to CRM/sheet
- Owner assignment rule applied
- Slack/email notification generated
- Task/deadline created

---

#### Step 4 — Follow-Up & Control (75 sec)
**Say:**
“We also automate follow-up logic so nothing goes cold. If no response in X hours, reminder sequences kick in.”

**Show:**
- Follow-up timer / status check
- Auto-reminder message
- Escalation rule placeholder

---

#### Step 5 — Reporting Outcome (60 sec)
**Say:**
“Every action is logged automatically, so your dashboard is always current.”

**Show:**
Simple KPI panel:
- New items today
- Response time
- Open vs completed
- Bottleneck stage

---

#### Step 6 — ROI Close (45 sec)
**Say:**
“This removes repetitive admin and gives your team faster execution with fewer mistakes. Based on your workflow, we’d start with [highest-impact step] first.”

**Close CTA:**
“Want us to map your exact process and deliver a launch plan this week?”

---

### Optional Discovery Questions (Post-Demo)
- Which step currently causes the most delays?
- Where do errors happen most often?
- How many hours/week are spent on manual updates?
- Which KPI do you wish you could trust daily?
