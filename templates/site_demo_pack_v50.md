## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

## Demo Goal
Show prospects exactly how NorthStar identifies a bottleneck, automates it, and produces measurable impact.

### Demo Setup (before call)
- Use a sample business scenario (lead intake + follow-up + weekly reporting)
- Open 3 tabs/windows:
  1. Intake source (form or spreadsheet)
  2. Automation logic view (workflow diagram or step list)
  3. Reporting output/dashboard snapshot

---

### Talk Track Script

**Step 1 — Frame the Problem (30–45 sec)**
“Most teams we work with are losing time in repetitive, error-prone handoffs — especially around intake, updates, and reporting.”

**Step 2 — Show Current-State Friction (30 sec)**
“Here’s the manual version: someone checks submissions, re-enters data, assigns follow-ups, and compiles status updates by hand.”

**Step 3 — Show Automated Flow (60–90 sec)**
“Now here’s the NorthStar version: as soon as a lead/form entry comes in, data is validated, categorized, routed to the right owner, and a follow-up sequence is triggered automatically.”

**Step 4 — Show Outcome Visibility (45–60 sec)**
“On the reporting side, this updates a live KPI view automatically — so leadership can see pipeline movement and response performance without waiting for manual reports.”

**Step 5 — Quantify ROI (30–45 sec)**
“For this type of workflow, teams commonly save 5–20 hours per week and reduce missed follow-ups significantly.”

**Step 6 — Close with Next Step (20–30 sec)**
“If this reflects your current bottleneck, we can run a free automation audit and give you a practical implementation roadmap.”

---

### Demo CTA Options
- Book Free Automation Audit
- Get a Custom Workflow Blueprint
- Request ROI Estimate
