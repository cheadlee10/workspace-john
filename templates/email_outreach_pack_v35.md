## Deliverability + Conversion Notes
- Avoid spam-heavy words ("guaranteed," "free money," "urgent act now").
- Keep paragraph spacing clean and skimmable.
- Use plain-text style for colder audiences.
- Prioritize relevance over cleverness in subject lines.
- Test 2–3 subject lines per segment and keep winners.
