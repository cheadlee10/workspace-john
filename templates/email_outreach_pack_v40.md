## 5) Fast Deployment Notes
- Keep first email under ~120 words when possible.
- Personalize first 1–2 lines with a real observation.
- Use one clear CTA only.
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 5, Day 9, Day 14.
- Stop after 4–5 touches unless engaged.
