## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (5–7 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual lead-to-follow-up process into an automated, trackable workflow.

### Audience
Owner/operator, operations manager, or sales lead at a small business.

### Setup (before call)
- Open a mock lead intake form
- Open CRM/list view
- Open dashboard view
- Prepare one “new lead” test entry

---

### Talk Track + Actions

#### Step 1 — Context (45 sec)
**Say:**
“Most teams we work with lose time at three points: lead intake, handoff, and follow-up. I’ll show you how we automate all three in one flow.”

#### Step 2 — Trigger the workflow (60 sec)
**Action:** Submit test lead via form.

**Say:**
“This form submission instantly creates a structured record, tags the lead, and routes it based on your rules—no manual copy/paste.”

#### Step 3 — Show routing + alerts (60 sec)
**Action:** Switch to CRM/pipeline and notification panel.

**Say:**
“Now the right person is assigned automatically, and if a lead sits too long, the system sends an escalation alert so nothing falls through.”

#### Step 4 — Show follow-up automation (75 sec)
**Action:** Open message timeline.

**Say:**
“Next, the lead receives a timed follow-up sequence—confirmation, reminder, and re-engagement if needed. Your team steps in only when it matters.”

#### Step 5 — Show reporting visibility (75 sec)
**Action:** Open KPI dashboard.

**Say:**
“Every interaction updates your dashboard in near real-time. You can track response time, conversion stages, and bottlenecks without manual reporting.”

#### Step 6 — Business outcome framing (45 sec)
**Say:**
“This is how clients typically recover hours per week, improve response consistency, and close more opportunities from the same lead volume.”

#### Step 7 — Close (45 sec)
**Say:**
“If this matches your process, we start with a workflow audit, identify your highest-ROI automation, and deliver phase one quickly.”

**CTA:**
“Want us to map your current process and show exactly what to automate first?”
