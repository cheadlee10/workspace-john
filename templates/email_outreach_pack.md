## 5 Cold Email Templates

### Cold Email 1 — Direct Value + Low-Friction CTA
**Subject line options:**
1. Quick idea for [Company]
2. [FirstName], 15-min fix to save your team hours
3. Small automation win for [Company]

**Email:**
Hi [FirstName],

I was looking at [Company] and noticed [Specific Observation].

We help businesses remove repetitive admin work with lightweight automations (reporting, lead routing, spreadsheet cleanup, follow-up triggers) so teams save time without replacing their current stack.

Most clients start seeing time savings in week 1.

Open to a quick 15-minute call this week to see if there’s a fit?

**CTA:** Reply with **“yes”** and I’ll send 2 time options.

---

### Cold Email 2 — Pain-First + Outcome Framing
**Subject line options:**
1. If your team is still doing this manually…
2. [FirstName], this may be costing [Company] weekly
3. Cut manual ops without new hires

**Email:**
Hi [FirstName],

If your team is still manually updating sheets, chasing status updates, or copying data between tools, you’re likely losing hours every week.

At NorthStar Synergy, we build practical automations that reduce bottlenecks fast—without forcing a full system overhaul.

- Fewer errors
- More capacity from the same team

**CTA:** Want me to send a one-page breakdown tailored to your workflow?
