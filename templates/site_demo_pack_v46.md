## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (for calls, Loom, or live page demo)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar turns a manual lead + ops workflow into an automated, trackable system in under 5 minutes.

### Demo Setup (what to show on screen)
- Form submission source (website form or intake form)
- CRM/pipeline board
- Team notification channel (email/Slack equivalent)
- Reporting dashboard view

### Script (talk track)
1. **Open with pain (20 sec):**  
   “Most teams lose time in the gap between inquiry, assignment, and follow-up. Here’s how we close that gap automatically.”

2. **Trigger event (30 sec):**  
   Submit a sample lead form with realistic data.

3. **Qualification + routing (45 sec):**  
   Show rule logic: score lead, tag service interest, assign owner based on territory or capacity.

4. **Instant follow-up (45 sec):**  
   Show confirmation email/SMS template trigger + next-step booking link.

5. **Ops task creation (45 sec):**  
   Show auto-created internal task/checklist with due dates and SLA reminder.

6. **Visibility layer (45 sec):**  
   Show dashboard update + alert for stalled leads or overdue tasks.

7. **Close with ROI (30 sec):**  
   “This flow alone typically cuts response time dramatically and removes repetitive admin work every day.”

### Demo CTA
**Primary:** Want this mapped to your process? Book a 20-minute Automation Audit.  
**Secondary:** Send us one workflow you hate—we’ll return a no-fluff automation plan.
