## 10) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

> Use this as a live walkthrough script for sales calls or recorded demos.

### Demo Goal
Show how one manual lead-to-follow-up workflow becomes automatic, trackable, and faster.

### Tools (example stack)
- Website form or inbox
- Automation layer (Zapier/Make/n8n)
- CRM/Sheet
- Slack/Email notifications

### Script
**Step 1 — Before (30–45 sec)**  
“Here’s the old process: a lead comes in, someone manually copies data, assigns ownership, sends follow-up, and hopes nothing gets missed.”

**Step 2 — Trigger Event (30 sec)**  
“Now I’ll submit a sample lead form. This starts the automation instantly.”

**Step 3 — Automated Actions (60–90 sec)**  
“As soon as that lead hits the system, it gets qualified, routed to the right pipeline, and a follow-up sequence is queued. The owner gets notified with context.”

**Step 4 — Visibility (45–60 sec)**  
“On the dashboard, you can see lead status, response timing, and conversion movement—without manual reporting work.”

**Step 5 — Business Impact (45 sec)**  
“This single flow cuts response lag, reduces dropped leads, and saves your team multiple admin hours each week.”

### Demo Close CTA
“If this looks useful, we can map your current process and show exactly where automation creates the fastest ROI.”

## 12) Visual/Design Notes (Builder-Friendly)

- Use short sections with generous white space.
- Keep card copy to 3–5 bullets for scanability.
- Pair every major claim with a proof element nearby.
- Use one primary accent color for CTA consistency.
- Add simple icons for each service card (lead, chart, gears, plug/integration).
