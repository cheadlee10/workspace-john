# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v55

Ready-to-send outreach assets for lead generation and appointment setting.

---

## Quick Use Guide
- Personalize: `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{pain_point}`, `{metric}`, `{calendar_link}`.
- Keep first touch under 120 words.
- Use one clear CTA per email.
- Send cadence suggestion: Day 1 (cold) → Day 3 (FU1) → Day 6 (FU2) → Day 10 (FU3) → Day 14 (FU4/FU5).

## 5 Cold Email Templates

### 1) Problem-First (Direct)
**Subject line options:**
- Quick idea for {company}
- Noticed this at {company}
- 15-min fix for {pain_point}

**Email:**
Hi {first_name},

Saw {company} and noticed a likely bottleneck around {pain_point}. Most teams we help are losing time/revenue there because the workflow is still manual or fragmented.

NorthStar Synergy builds lightweight automation + process systems that cut turnaround time and increase output without adding headcount.

If useful, I can send a 3-point teardown of what I’d optimize first at {company}.

**CTA:** Open to a quick 15-minute call this week? {calendar_link}

### 3) Audit Offer (Low Friction)
**Subject line options:**

## Personalization Prompts (Before Sending)
- Mention one real observation from their site/offer/process.
- Reference one likely bottleneck by name.
- Tie expected result to a metric they care about (speed, conversion, bookings, revenue).
- Keep tone conversational and specific.
