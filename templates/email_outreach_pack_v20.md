## Fast Send Checklist (Before You Hit Send)
- Personalize first line with a real observation
- Use one clear CTA (don’t stack asks)
- Keep email under 140 words when possible
- Send follow-ups at 48–72 hour intervals
- Stop after 4–5 total touches if no signal
