# QUICK REFERENCE: SENDING RULES

## Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 9-11am local time (primary), 1-2pm (secondary)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

## Cadence
| Email | Day | Purpose |
|-------|-----|---------|
| Initial | 0 | Hook + observation + proof + visual/demo |
| Follow-up 1 | 3 | Gentle bump + new insight |
| Follow-up 2 | 10 | New proof/case study |
| Breakup | 17 | Graceful exit |

## Non-Negotiables
- [ ] Under 100 words
- [ ] One specific observation proves you researched
- [ ] No pricing in first touch
- [ ] Soft CTA only
- [ ] Visual/proof link included (Sequence 1) or offer to show demo
- [ ] Plain text — paste through Notepad first
- [ ] No smart quotes (verify no "?" characters)
- [ ] All {{variables}} replaced with real data

# A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK

## Subject Line Tests
Run 50/50 splits on:
1. Question vs. Statement (`Quick question about X` vs. `Idea for X`)
2. Company name vs. First name (`{{CompanyName}} + Google` vs. `{{FirstName}}, quick thought?`)
3. Specific vs. Curiosity (`Missing calls in {{City}}?` vs. `Noticed something`)

## CTA Tests
Run 50/50 splits on:
1. Soft vs. Direct (`Worth a look?` vs. `Want me to call you this week?`)
2. Time-bounded vs. Open (`Can I send a 2-minute video?` vs. `Happy to show you`)

## Track
- Open rate (subject line performance)
- Reply rate (body/CTA performance)
- Positive reply rate (offer resonance)
- Meeting booked rate (qualification quality)
