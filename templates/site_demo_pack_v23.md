## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (5–7 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy identifies a manual bottleneck and replaces it with a live, practical automation flow.

### Audience
Business owners, operations managers, team leads.

---

### Step 1 — Problem Framing (1 min)
**Script:**  
“Most teams don’t have a software problem—they have a workflow bottleneck problem. Today I’ll show a common example: lead intake, follow-up, and status tracking done manually across forms, inboxes, and spreadsheets.”

**On-screen:**  
Before-state diagram: Form → Email inbox → Spreadsheet copy/paste → Delayed follow-up.

---

### Step 2 — Current-State Pain (1 min)
**Script:**  
“In this manual flow, leads sit in inboxes, follow-ups are inconsistent, and reporting is always behind. That means slower response times and missed opportunities.”

**On-screen:**  
Pain indicators: missed SLA, duplicate entries, no ownership visibility.

---

### Step 3 — Automation Overview (1 min)
**Script:**  
“Now here’s the automated version: a new lead triggers routing, owner assignment, follow-up task creation, and pipeline status updates instantly.”

**On-screen:**  
After-state diagram: Form submission → automation engine → CRM update → task/reminder → dashboard refresh.

---

### Step 4 — Live Walkthrough (2 min)
**Script:**  
“Let’s submit one sample lead. Watch what happens: owner assigned, contact tagged, follow-up task created, and status reflected in the pipeline dashboard—without manual handoffs.”

**On-screen actions:**
1. Submit demo lead form
2. Show instant record creation in CRM/table
3. Show follow-up task/reminder generated
4. Show dashboard counter/KPI update

---

### Step 5 — Outcome + ROI Framing (1 min)
**Script:**  
“This is how we reduce admin overhead and increase response speed. The result is a team that spends less time managing process and more time closing work.”

**On-screen:**  
Outcome summary panel:
- Time saved per week: [X hours]
- Response speed improvement: [X%]
- Manual touchpoints removed: [X]

---
