## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy converts a manual process into an automated, measurable workflow.

### Demo Setup (Before Call)
- Open a “Before” spreadsheet with manual steps highlighted
- Open “After” dashboard with auto-refresh indicators
- Prepare one sample lead submission form
- Keep a short results summary visible (time saved, error reduction)

---

### Script

#### Step 1 — Frame the Problem (30–45 sec)
“Most teams we work with are stuck in copy/paste loops—updating sheets manually, chasing follow-ups, and rebuilding reports every week.”

#### Step 2 — Show the Manual Baseline (45–60 sec)
“Here’s the old process: data comes in here, someone re-formats it, then updates two other tabs, then sends a status email. This repeats daily.”

#### Step 3 — Trigger the Automation (45–60 sec)
“Now I’ll submit one lead record. Watch the flow: it’s logged, tagged, routed, and reflected in the dashboard automatically.”

#### Step 4 — Verify Outcomes (45–60 sec)
“Within seconds, the dashboard updates, owner assignment is set, and follow-up status is tracked—without manual intervention.”

#### Step 5 — Tie to Business Impact (30–45 sec)
“This is where teams usually recover multiple hours per week and improve response consistency, which directly impacts conversion and client experience.”

#### Step 6 — Close with Clear Next Action (20–30 sec)
“If useful, we can run a free workflow review and map one high-friction process in your business. You’ll get a concrete automation plan and timeline.”

**Close CTA:** Book Free Workflow Review
