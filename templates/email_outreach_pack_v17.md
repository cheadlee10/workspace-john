## Recommended Sending Notes (Internal)
- Keep first emails under 120–150 words when possible.
- Use 1 clear CTA per message.
- Send follow-ups every 2–4 business days.
- Personalize first line using public context (recent post, page update, local market note).
- Track replies by intent: interested / not now / referral / not a fit.
