## Sending Notes (Immediate Use)

- Keep first email under ~120 words when possible.
- Use one clear CTA only.
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 4, Day 7, Day 10, Day 14.
- Rotate subject lines to reduce pattern filtering.
- Stop after breakup email unless prospect re-engages.
