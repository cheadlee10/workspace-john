## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy turns one manual workflow into an automated, trackable system that saves time and improves response consistency.

### Recommended Demo Scenario
**Use Case:** New lead submits a web form → record is enriched/tagged → follow-up is triggered → team is notified → dashboard updates.

### Script Outline

#### Step 1 — Problem Framing (30–45 sec)
**Talk Track:**  
“Most teams lose time in the same places: copy/paste admin, delayed follow-up, and scattered updates across tools. Here’s a simple before/after of that exact issue.”

#### Step 2 — Show Manual ‘Before’ (30–45 sec)
**Show:**
- Spreadsheet with manual lead entries
- Email/slack reminders handled by memory
- No clear ownership or SLA

**Talk Track:**  
“In this version, each lead requires multiple manual touches. Follow-up is inconsistent, and performance is hard to measure.”

#### Step 3 — Trigger the Automation (45–60 sec)
**Show:**
- Submit sample lead form
- Automation platform run starts
- Lead appears in CRM/Sheet with tags

**Talk Track:**  
“One submission now triggers the entire sequence automatically—capture, route, and qualification tags are applied in seconds.”

#### Step 4 — Follow-Up + Team Handoff (45–60 sec)
**Show:**
- Auto-follow-up email/SMS drafted/sent
- Slack/Teams notification to owner
- Task assigned with due date

**Talk Track:**  
“The system sends immediate follow-up, alerts the right owner, and creates a task so nothing gets dropped.”

#### Step 5 — Visibility & ROI (45–60 sec)
**Show:**
- KPI dashboard updated (new lead count, response time)
- Weekly summary report example

**Talk Track:**  
“Now the process is measurable. You can see response speed, handoff quality, and conversion opportunities in one place.”

#### Step 6 — Close + CTA (20–30 sec)
**Talk Track:**  
“If this is one bottleneck in your business, we can map and launch a practical version quickly. Bring us one process, and we’ll show you the highest-ROI first automation.”

**CTA Button:** Book Free Workflow Audit
