## Usage Notes (Immediate Deployment)
1. Pick one template + one subject line variant.
2. Personalize `[RecentObservation]` with a specific detail (improves reply rate).
3. Keep first touch under 120 words when possible.
4. Send follow-ups every 2–4 business days.
5. Stop after 5 follow-ups unless prospect re-engages.
