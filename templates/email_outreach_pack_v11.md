# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v11

High-converting outreach assets built for cold prospecting, re-engagement, and niche targeting.

---

## Quick Use Guide
- Personalize at least 2 fields per email: `[First Name]`, `[Company]`, `[Specific Observation]`, `[Relevant Pain]`.
- Keep first-touch emails 80–130 words.
- Use one clear CTA only.
- Follow-up cadence: Day 2, Day 4, Day 7, Day 10, Day 14.
- If no response after Follow-Up 5, recycle lead in 30–45 days with a new angle.
