# NorthStar Synergy — Email Outreach Pack v22

Ready-to-send outbound templates designed for small-to-mid businesses needing automation, lead handling, and revenue ops support.

---

## How to Use (Fast)
- Personalize: `{first_name}`, `{company}`, `{pain_point}`, `{your_name}`, `{calendar_link}`
- Keep each send under ~120 words when possible
- Include one clear CTA only
- Send follow-ups 2, 4, 7, 10, and 14 days after initial email

---

## 5 Cold Email Templates

### 1) Pain-Point + Quick Win
**Subject Line Options:**
- Quick idea for {company}
- {first_name}, noticed a gap you can fix this week
- Simple way to capture more inbound leads

**Email Body:**
Hi {first_name},

I was reviewing {company} and noticed a common growth leak: {pain_point}.

NorthStar Synergy helps teams fix this with lightweight automations (lead routing, follow-up triggers, reporting) that usually go live in days—not months.

Most clients start by recovering missed leads and cutting manual admin time 20–40%.

If you’re open, I can send a 3-step breakdown tailored to your current setup.

**CTA:** Reply with “send it” and I’ll share the exact plan.
