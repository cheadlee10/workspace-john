## 8) Simple Demo Flow Script (3–5 Minutes)

### Demo Goal
Show a clear before/after on a common operations flow: **lead intake → assignment → follow-up → reporting**.

### Demo Assets to Prepare
- Sample intake form (or web lead form)
- Spreadsheet/CRM with mock lead records
- Automation scenario already connected (hidden until trigger)
- Live KPI dashboard showing lead status and response-time fields

### Live Walkthrough Script

**Step 1 — Context + Manual Pain (30–45 sec)**  
“Here’s the current manual path: a lead arrives, someone checks the sheet, assigns an owner, sends a follow-up, and updates status. That creates delays, inconsistency, and dropped handoffs.”

**Step 2 — Trigger the Workflow (20–30 sec)**  
“Let’s submit one new lead now.”  
(Submit demo lead in real time.)

**Step 3 — Show Automation Sequence (60–90 sec)**  
“Now watch the system run: the lead is categorized, routed to the correct owner, follow-up is generated, and the dashboard updates automatically.”

**Step 4 — Show Guardrails (45–60 sec)**  
“If required fields are missing or urgency is high, the workflow flags it and alerts the right person instead of silently failing.”

**Step 5 — Quantify the Outcome (45–60 sec)**  
“This removes manual touchpoints, shortens response time, and gives leadership live visibility into pipeline health.”

### Demo Close (15–30 sec)
“If this resembles your current process, we can map your exact workflow in a short audit and prioritize the fastest ROI automations first.”
