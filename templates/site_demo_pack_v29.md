## 4) Simple Demo Flow Script (2–4 Minutes)

### Goal
Show how NorthStar Synergy turns one manual bottleneck into a reliable, measurable automation.

### Demo Setup (before call)
- Open a sample intake form (new lead/customer request)
- Open CRM/pipeline view
- Open task board or notification channel
- Open reporting dashboard

### Script

**Step 1 — Problem Framing (20–30 sec)**
“Most teams lose time when a request comes in and someone has to manually copy data, assign tasks, and send updates. We’ll show this end-to-end in under 2 minutes.”

**Step 2 — Trigger Event (20 sec)**
Submit sample form entry live.

Narration:
“This form submission is the trigger. From here, no manual copy/paste is needed.”

**Step 3 — Automated Routing (30–45 sec)**
Show new record auto-created in CRM with tags/priority.
Show owner assignment or route based on rules.

Narration:
“Rules assign ownership instantly based on service type, urgency, or geography.”

**Step 4 — Task & Notification (30–45 sec)**
Show task auto-created in project board.
Show Slack/email notification to assigned owner.

Narration:
“The right person is notified immediately, with context and next actions attached.”

**Step 5 — Reporting Update (30–45 sec)**
Show dashboard/KPI counter update.

Narration:
“Leadership gets live visibility without waiting for weekly manual reports.”

**Step 6 — Outcome Wrap (20–30 sec)**
“Same workflow, less delay, fewer errors, and clearer accountability. This is the type of automation we customize around your exact process.”

### Close CTA (10 sec)
“If you share one recurring process, we’ll map a practical automation plan with timeline and fixed pricing.”
