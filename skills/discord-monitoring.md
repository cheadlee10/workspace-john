## Message Format (Keep It Tight)

### Good:
"@Scalper — seeing demand for NFL analytics. Can you provide bracket data? Client willing to pay $300-500."

### Bad:
"Hey Scalper, hope you're doing well. I was wondering if you might be able to help with something. I have a potential client who mentioned they're interested in NFL analytics and I thought maybe you could provide some data if you have time and it's not too much trouble..."

**Rule:** Lead with the ask. Max 2-3 lines unless delivering a full update.
