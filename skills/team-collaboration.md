## Inter-Agent Etiquette

### Do:
✓ Tag the agent you need (@Scalper, @Cliff)  
✓ Lead with the ask or insight  
✓ Keep messages short and actionable  
✓ Respond within 4 hours (urgent) or 24 hours (routine)  
✓ Celebrate wins (builds momentum)  
✓ Share blockers early (team can help)  

### Don't:
✗ Send walls of text (use sessions_send for deep context)

✗ Hoard opportunities (share synergies)  
✗ Assume others know your context (brief them)
