## SECTION 1: THE NEW FIVERR ALGORITHM (2024-2026)

### Success Score System (THE GAME CHANGER)

**What It Is:**
- Hidden 10-point rating per gig
- **Relative ranking** — you're compared to others in your subcategory
- Evaluated daily (not monthly)
- Heavily impacts visibility

**Six Key Factors (Estimated Weights):**

| Factor | Weight | What Fiverr Measures | How to Win |
|--------|--------|---------------------|-----------|
| **Buyer Satisfaction** | 35% | Overall experience rating | Over-deliver, communicate proactively |
| **Value for Money** | 25% | Price vs quality perception | Competitive pricing + extras |
| **Communication Quality** | 20% | Professionalism, clarity, speed | <1h response, clear messages |
| **Delivery Timeliness** | 15% | On-time delivery rate | Deliver early when possible |
| **Repeat Buyer Rate** | 5% | % of returning clients | Build relationships, upsell |

**Critical Insight:** Your Success Score is **RELATIVE to your subcategory**. A 7/10 in "Excel Automation" might rank higher than 8/10 in "Data Entry" because competition quality differs.

**How to Check Your Score:**
- Go to Gig Analytics → Success Score tab
- Shows per-gig score (not overall)
- **Below 8.0 = severely reduced visibility**
- **8.0-8.9 = competitive**
- **9.0+ = top tier ranking**

### Dynamic Ranking System

**How It Works:**
```
Hour 1: Your gig ranks #3 in search → Gets clicks → Fiverr tests conversion
Hour 2: CTR is 2.8% (good) → Moves to #2
Hour 3: CTR drops to 1.5% (bad) → Drops to #15
Hour 4: New buyer orders → Bumps back to #5
Hour 5: Competitor gets more orders → You drop to #8
```

**Key Takeaway:** Ranking is NEVER stable. It's constant A/B testing by Fiverr.

**What This Means:**
- Can't coast on good ranking
- Must maintain high CTR + conversion daily
- One bad review = instant visibility drop
- Platform rewards CURRENT performance, not past success

## SECTION 9: CRISIS MANAGEMENT

### Bad Review Prevention

**Before It Happens:**
1. Set clear expectations (delivery scope, timeline)
2. Communicate proactively (updates every 24h)
3. Deliver early if possible
4. Include revision in scope (don't make them ask)
5. Check quality before delivery (QC checklist)

**If Client Unhappy:**
1. Respond within 1 hour: "I want to make this right."
2. Offer immediate revision (no questions asked)
3. Over-deliver on revision (add extras)
4. If still unhappy: offer partial refund
5. Learn from feedback (update process)

**After Bad Review:**
1. Respond professionally (public reply)
2. Show you care, don't argue
3. Implement fix to prevent next time
4. Earn 5+ more 5-star reviews to dilute impact

### Order Cancellation Request

**Strategy:**
1. Try to resolve first: "What would make this right?"
2. Offer revision, discount, or extra work
3. If client insists on cancel: agree politely
4. **Don't argue** — cancellation hurts, arguing hurts more
5. Document what went wrong → fix process

### Running Behind Schedule

**Protocol:**
1. Message client ASAP (before deadline)
2. Brief explanation (no excuses)
3. Request 24-48h extension
4. Deliver early on extension
5. Include small extra as apology
