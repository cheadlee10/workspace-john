# Quick Deploy - 2 Clicks to Live Site

## Step 1: Deploy Site (Gets Working URL Immediately)

**Open PowerShell in this folder, run:**
```powershell
npx vercel --prod --yes
```

**It will ask for login** - open browser link, authorize.

**Result:** Site live at `https://northstar-synergy-xxx.vercel.app` in 2 minutes

---

## Step 2: Add Custom Domain (Payment Required)

**After Step 1 completes, it will show your project URL.**

**Go to:** https://vercel.com/dashboard

**Click:** Your project (northstar-synergy)

**Click:** Settings → Domains

**Enter:** northstar-synergy.dev

**Click:** Add

**Vercel will prompt:** Purchase for $15

**Enter payment info** → Domain live in 5 minutes
