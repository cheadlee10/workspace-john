# NEW Pricing Strategy (Monthly Subscriptions)

## The Shift

**OLD:** One-time project fees ($499, $1,999, $4,999)
**NEW:** Monthly subscriptions ($99-999/month)

**Why:** 
- Higher LTV (Customer Lifetime Value)
- Predictable MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Better for customers (lower commitment, ongoing support)
- Industry standard for automation/SaaS
