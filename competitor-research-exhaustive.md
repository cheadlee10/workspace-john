# 1. BENTOBOX (getbento.com)

- **Custom Restaurant Websites** - Professional, mobile-responsive sites with full-bleed photography, custom fonts/colors/layouts

- **Google Sync (Local Sync)** - Push menu updates directly to Google local listings
- **Customization Package** - Enhanced design options beyond templates

### SELL (Commerce)
- **Online Ordering / Takeout & Delivery** - Commission-free direct ordering with real-time POS sync
- **Online Catering (Pre-Order)** - Catering management system ($79/mo add-on)
- **Dine-in Ordering** - In-restaurant ordering via mobile
- **QR Code Order & Pay** - Contactless table ordering
- **eCommerce Stores** - Sell merchandise (apparel, cocktail kits, condiments, cookbooks) with no third-party fees
- **Gift Cards** - Digital and physical; customizable amounts; redeemable online during checkout; promotional gift card sending
- **Merchandise** - Dedicated storefront with size/color selectors and promo codes
- **Ticketing & Events Management** - Event creation, ticket sales, event marketing

### ENGAGE (Marketing)
- **Reservations** - Direct reservation widget, no cover fees, Reserve with Google integration, auto-syncs to diner database
- **Digital Waitlist** - Virtual queue management with algorithm-based wait time estimates; guests join online, in-person, or by phone; two-way SMS; post-dining surveys; VIP tagging
- **Table Management** - Real-time table state tracking via iPad; capacity management; auto-updates when guests pay via Clover POS
- **Email Marketing** - Automated personalization based on customer data; integrations with Mailchimp, Emma, Constant Contact, Campaign Monitor
- **SMS Marketing** - Direct text message campaigns
- **SEO** - Built-in SEO fields in sitemaps and page structures
- **Loyalty Programs** - Customer reward programs
- **Marketing Tools** - Comprehensive suite with diner data

### OPERATE (Backend)
- **BentoCore** - Central hub housing all diner data, menu info, branding materials
- **Diner Database** - Complete guest profiles with history across online and in-house interactions; custom tags (VIP, preferences)
- **Menu Management** - Centralized menu updates across all channels
- **Brand Center** - Centralized branding materials

### Integrations

| Takeout & Delivery Add-on | $49/mo |
| Online Catering Add-on | $79/mo |
| Foundations Plan | $279/mo |
| Signature Plan | $479/mo |

## What Customers LOVE
- Ease of use for non-technical staff ("easy to make updates for staff who are not tech savvy")
- Outstanding customer support ("answers promptly, thoroughly, and personably")
- Seamless content management
- All-in-one platform (ordering, events, social media, marketing in one place)
- Restaurant-specific features that just work
- Beautiful, design-forward websites
- Affordability relative to value

## What Customers HATE
- **Billing issues** - Sold at one price, billed a higher price; confusing billing
- **Limited customization** - Users with web experience find flexibility lacking; "very limited in advanced changes"
- **Template/scalability limitations** - Changing templates causes issues; scaling can be a hassle
- **SEO not automatic** - Platform doesn't do SEO for you out of the box
- **Feature duplication costs** - If you already have CRM/gift card/reservation systems, you pay for unused BentoBox versions
- **Everything is extra** - Add-on costs stack up quickly

## USP (Unique Selling Point)
Beautiful, design-forward restaurant websites with deep dine-in management (reservations, waitlist, table management). The most "hospitality-focused" platform with features like VIP tagging and two-way SMS.

## Biggest Weakness to Exploit
Limited customization for tech-savvy users. Expensive when you add all modules. No AI features. No branded mobile app. No phone answering. Design-focused but not conversion-optimized.

# 3. OWNER.COM

## Complete Feature List

### Core Products
- **AI Website Builder** - AI-optimized, conversion-focused design; proven template personalized with restaurant branding; launches within ~1 week; domain stays with restaurant
- **Online Ordering System** - Familiar UX modeled after major delivery apps; zero commission to restaurant; 5% customer order fee
- **Zero-Commission Delivery** - Top-rated drivers at flat fee (~$7/delivery); no commission
- **Custom Branded Mobile App** - 5-star rated iOS/Android app; saved favorites, payment info, order tracking; rewards built in
- **Automated Marketing** - Pre-made automated email and text campaigns; AI-enabled email marketer
- **Loyalty & Rewards Program** - Points-based system; no username/password needed (phone + email + name); adjustable point values; sign-up offer; track redemptions like CRM
- **AI Restaurant Grader** - Free tool to scan restaurant's online presence; competitor analysis; error identification; recommendations for improvement

### Key Technical Differentiators
- AI continuously improves SEO, marketing, and ordering
- 2x higher conversion rate than typical restaurant websites
- "Sales-first" design philosophy over customization
- Pre-made marketing campaigns based on best practices from thousands of restaurants
- Customers keep all their data if they leave Owner

### Integrations
- **POS:** Square (native), Clover (native), Toast (via Otter), many others via Otter
- **Payments:** Stripe, Google Pay, Apple Pay
- **Alternative:** Free tablet or printer provided if POS integration not preferred

## Pricing
| Component | Cost |
|-----------|------|
| Monthly Subscription | $499/mo |
| Commission on Orders | 0% |
| Customer Order Fee | 5% per order (paid by customer) |
| Delivery Cost | ~$7 flat per delivery |
| Contract | Month-to-month, no cancellation fees |
| Setup | Included; launch within ~1 week |

**Hidden Cost Reality:** Some restaurant owners report that while the $499/mo is the restaurant's cost, the 5% customer surcharge on all orders can total $400-$500/week, meaning customers collectively pay $5,000-$6,000/month in fees.

## What Customers LOVE
- Combines website, ordering, marketing, CRM, app in one place
- "Levels the playing field for independent restaurants" vs. big chains
- Exceptional ease of use
- Quick setup (within a week)
- Outstanding customer support (500+ five-star reviews on G2/Capterra)
- Significant increase in direct online sales ($120k+/mo for some)
- No long-term contracts

## What Customers HATE
- **5% customer surcharge** - Customers see and pay this fee; can feel misleading when marketed as "commission-free"
- **Sales drops during transition** - Some report $6,000/month drops initially; told to wait 3 months
- **Limited design freedom** - Owner explicitly says "If you're looking for a lot of design freedom, Owner is not the right fit"
- **Misleading "commission-free" marketing** - Technically true (restaurant pays $0 commission) but customer pays 5%
- **POS integration limitations** - Native integrations only with Square and Clover; others via Otter

## USP (Unique Selling Point)
Branded mobile app with built-in loyalty program. AI-optimized conversion-focused design that gets 2x more conversions than typical restaurant websites. All-in-one platform with genuinely simple $499/mo flat pricing. The only platform with its own branded app included.

## Biggest Weakness to Exploit
5% customer surcharge is unpopular and feels deceptive. Limited design customization. Transition period can lose restaurants money. POS integration gaps (relies on Otter for most). No reservation/waitlist/table management features. No event management.

Auto-generate a full month of email, SMS, and social media content based on the restaurant's menu, events, and reviews. Operators just review/approve. This is the biggest time-saver for busy restaurant owners.

White-label iOS/Android app with rewards program, saved favorites, order tracking, push notifications. Owner.com's biggest differentiator. Drives 85%+ increase in reorders.

# 17. GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

## What NOBODY Does Well
1. **Unified AI across all functions** - No platform truly unifies AI for website building, marketing, phone answering, ordering optimization, and analytics in one coherent system
2. **Real-time competitive intelligence** - No platform shows restaurants how they compare to local competitors in real-time
3. **Dynamic pricing** - Only Olo touches this for enterprise; nobody offers dynamic menu pricing for independents
4. **Multi-language support** - Most platforms are English-only for website content (Menufy has multilingual support, but for customer service only)
5. **Staff-facing features** - Few platforms help with hiring, training, scheduling alongside marketing
6. **Video-first content** - Nobody auto-generates video content from photos/menu data
7. **Social proof integration** - Nobody dynamically pulls and displays real-time social media content on the restaurant website
8. **Predictive ordering** - Nobody uses historical data to predict what a returning customer wants
9. **Table-specific upselling** - Nobody uses data to suggest items when a dine-in guest scans a QR code based on what others at their table ordered
10. **Integrated tipping/review prompts** - Post-order tip and review solicitation is clunky across all platforms

## Pricing Opportunity
- BentoBox: $119-$479/mo (website only, add-ons extra)
- Popmenu: $179-$499/mo (add-ons push to $1,000+)
- Owner.com: $499/mo flat (but 5% customer fee)
- ChowNow: $199/mo + setup fees
- SpotHopper: Not disclosed (marketing only)
- Square: $0-$149/mo (basic features)
- GloriaFood: $0-$166/mo (very basic)

**Opportunity:** A platform at $199-$349/mo that includes website + ordering + branded app + AI marketing + loyalty WITHOUT hidden per-order fees or 5% customer surcharges would be extremely competitive. No one currently offers this value proposition.

## Distribution Opportunity
SpotHopper's partnerships with US Foods and Dining Alliance give them access to restaurant owners through their existing vendor relationships. A similar partnership with food distributors, POS companies, or restaurant associations could be a massive growth channel.
