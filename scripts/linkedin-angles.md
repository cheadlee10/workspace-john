# LinkedIn Prospecting Angles
*High-ticket, warm leads. Best for retainers + consulting work.*

## Target Profiles

### Angle 1: Business Owners Overwhelmed by Data
**Profile:** Small business owner, 10-100 employees, complaining about manual work
**Trigger Posts:** 
- "Spending too much time on [manual task]"
- "Wish I had time to [business activity] instead of spreadsheets"
- "Our process is broken, need solutions"

**Pitch:** "I automate that. Built [X systems] that freed up teams. Worth a chat?"

**Service Fit:** Excel Automation, Data Engineering, Reporting Pipeline
**Typical Deal:** $500-2000 (small to medium)

---

### Angle 2: Operations Managers / Finance Teams
**Profile:** Ops manager, finance director, CFO
**Trigger Posts:**
- Talking about process improvement
- Mentioning team efficiency
- Discussing tools/software
- Budget/planning discussions

**Pitch:** "I build systems to handle that. Automated P&L reporting for [industry] saved [X] team [X hours]. Open to exploring?"

**Service Fit:** Reporting Pipeline, Excel Audit, Data Engineering
**Typical Deal:** $1000-5000 (medium to high)

---

### Angle 3: Tech Leads / CTO at Early-Stage Startups
**Profile:** CTO, tech lead, engineering manager, startup founder
**Trigger Posts:**
- "Building [product]"
- "Looking for freelance developers"
- "Need help with [technical problem]"

**Pitch:** "I can help with that. Built [X projects] in [tech stack]. Quick scope call?"

**Service Fit:** Web Development, API Integration, Custom Bots, Full-Stack
**Typical Deal:** $2000-10000 (high-ticket, retainer potential)

---

### Angle 4: E-Commerce / Marketplace Owners
**Profile:** Shopify owner, Amazon seller, marketplace founder
**Trigger Posts:**
- "Scaling our business"
- Mentioning inventory, pricing, competitor analysis
- Automation discussion

**Pitch:** "I automate inventory + pricing. Python script tracks [X competitors], alerts on pricing gaps. Worth exploring?"

**Service Fit:** Python Scripts, Data Engineering, Web Scraping, Reporting
**Typical Deal:** $300-2000 (medium, retainer potential)

---

### Angle 5: Marketing/Content Creators
**Profile:** Marketing agency owner, content creator, marketing manager
**Trigger Posts:**
- "Scaling our processes"
- Mentioning tools/automation
- Client management discussion

**Pitch:** "I build systems to handle client work at scale. Custom forms, data collection, reporting. Let's chat?"

**Service Fit:** Web Development, Custom Forms, Automation, Bots
**Typical Deal:** $500-3000 (medium to high)

## Cold Message Structure (LinkedIn)

### Connection Request
Personalized + specific (never generic):

"Hey [Name], saw your post about [specific thing]. I specialize in solving exactly that for [their industry]. Would love to connect."

→ 60%+ acceptance rate if specific
→ 20% acceptance rate if generic

### Follow-Up After Connection
Wait 2-3 days, then:

"Hey [Name], thanks for connecting. Quick question — [specific about their challenge]. I've helped [X similar companies/people] with [result]. Worth a quick call?"

→ Keep it brief (3 sentences max)
→ Ask for something small (15-min call, not "let's do business")
→ Lead with their problem, not your service

### If They Engage
Book a 15-min call via calendar link.

## Daily LinkedIn Workflow (15 min)

1. **Search** (3 min): Keywords like "business owner", "operations", "finance", "startup", "e-commerce"
2. **Filter** (2 min): Recent activity + posts about problems you solve
3. **Add** (5 min): Send 3-5 personalized connection requests
4. **Follow-up** (3 min): Reply to any new messages/engagement
5. **Log** (2 min): Add new prospects to `leads.jsonl`

## LinkedIn Content Strategy (Optional)
Post 1-2x per week:
- **Monday:** Problem-solution post ("3 ways to automate spreadsheet work")
- **Wednesday:** Case study or win ("Built X for startup Y, saved them Z")
- **Friday:** Advice/insight ("Most freelancers miss this in proposals")

→ Builds social proof
→ Attracts inbound leads
→ Positions you as expert

## Messaging Scripts by Angle

### For Overworked Business Owners
"Hey [Name], I saw your post about [manual task]. I build systems that eliminate that kind of work. Built reporting pipeline for [similar company], went from 4 hours manual → 15 min auto. Open to exploring?"

### For Operations Managers
"Hey [Name], cool to see [company] scaling. Quick question — how do you currently handle [process]? I've automated that for [similar orgs] and usually find 10-20 hours of saved work per month. Worth chatting?"

### For CTOs / Tech Leads
"Hey [Name], saw you're working on [project]. I specialize in [relevant tech]. Built similar for [startup], shipped in [timeframe]. Interested in exploring a partnership?"

### For E-Commerce / Marketplace
"Hey [Name], saw [company] is scaling. I build automation for [specific problem: pricing, inventory, competitor tracking]. Helped [similar seller] find $[X] in pricing opportunities. Want to explore?"

## Key Metrics to Track
- **Contacts sent:** 5-10/week
- **Acceptance rate:** 60%+ (personalized) vs 20% (generic)
- **Message response rate:** 30%+
- **Call booking rate:** 50% of responses
- **Deal close rate:** 30% of calls
- **Average deal:** $1000-3000

**Weekly goal:** 5 contacts → 3 accept → 1 call → 1 proposal → $1000-3000 deal

---

## Anti-Patterns
❌ Copy-paste generic messages (0% response)
❌ Pitch immediately after connection (40% rejection)
❌ Long-winded follow-ups (low response)
❌ No clear CTA ("feel free to reach out")
❌ Multiple messages before they respond (pushy)

→ Personalized + specific + brief + clear CTA = highest ROI
