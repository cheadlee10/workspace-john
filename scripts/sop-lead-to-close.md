# SOP — Lead to Close to Delivery (No-Site Local Businesses)

## Purpose
Standardize execution from first touch to live site launch.

## Step 0 — Pre-Flight (daily)
- Open/update `leads.jsonl` and `jobs.jsonl`
- Confirm today’s outreach targets (20 leads minimum)
- Prepare call script + email template + objection sheet

## Step 1 — Prospecting (30–60 min)
1. Search Google Maps/Yelp/Yellow Pages by city + niche
2. Mark website status:
   - `none` = highest priority
   - `facebook_only` = high priority
   - `outdated` = upgrade target
3. Add lead record with status `new`

## Step 2 — First Touch (same day)
- Run call first where number exists
- If no answer, leave voicemail + send follow-up email/text
- Set status `contacted`
- Schedule next action date (D+1)

## Step 3 — Qualification (2–3 min)
Ask:
1. How do customers currently find you?
2. Do you have a website now?
3. What outcome matters most? (calls/bookings/walk-ins)
4. Who makes final decision?
5. If this makes sense, how soon do you want to launch?

Disqualify if no decision-maker access + no urgency + no budget fit.

## Step 4 — Demo Booking
Goal: book 10-minute call within 24–48 hours.
Booking line:
"I can show examples and exact pricing in 10 minutes. Is [time A] or [time B] better?"

Update lead status `proposal_sent` only after live quote is sent.

## Step 5 — Demo Structure (10 min)
1. 60 sec: recap their problem in their words
2. 2 min: show similar example outcome
3. 3 min: present Good/Better/Best tiers
4. 2 min: objection handling
5. 2 min: close + next step + timeline

## Step 6 — Pricing + Proposal
- Recommend Tier 2 anchor first
- If price pushback: reduce scope, not rate
- Send concise proposal (same day)
- Update status `negotiating`

## Step 7 — Close + Payment Commitment
Closing line:
"Best fit is [Tier]. If we start now, you’ll be live by [date]. Want me to lock that in?"

When verbal yes:
- Confirm tier, timeline, and required assets
- Add job record in `jobs.jsonl` with status `quoted`/`in_progress`

## Step 8 — Internal Handoff to Cliff
Send one-page brief:
- Client + tier + promised date
- Site structure/features
- Brand assets/content links
- Contact/booking/payment requirements
- Revision expectations

## Step 9 — Delivery + Review
- Client review call
- 1–2 revision rounds
- Final launch approval
- Mark job `completed`

## Step 10 — Post-Launch Monetization
Within 24 hours of launch:
- Offer maintenance plan ($100/$200/$300)
- Ask for testimonial/case-study permission
- Schedule 30-day check-in

## Follow-Up Cadence
- **D+1:** “Wanted to bump this in case timing is better today.”
- **D+5:** “Can hold your launch slot this week if helpful.”
- **D+10:** “Last follow-up — close file or quick 10-min review?”

After D+10 with no response: mark `lost` and move on.
