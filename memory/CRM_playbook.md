CRM Outreach Playbook — NorthStar Synergy
Goal: Generate qualified leads, quotes, and closed deals from CRM data (leads.jsonl, jobs.jsonl, CRM summaries).
Cadence: 6-touch sequence over 14 days; repeat monthly with tweaks. Track in jobs.jsonl/leads.jsonl.
Key metrics: leads generated/week, quotes sent/week, conversion rate, average deal size, win rate, time to close, churn/retention.

1) Targeting & Qualification
- Source: current CRM leads.jsonl and jobs.jsonl.
- Qualification criteria: budget >= good tier; authority confirmed; need aligns with services; timeline within 30-60 days.
- Stage gates: New -> Contacted -> Proposal -> Negotiation -> Closed/Won or Lost.

2) Cadence (outreach sequence)
- Day 0: Initial email (value-first, ROI framing) + LinkedIn note (optional) + call script ready.
- Day 2: Follow-up 1 (anchor ROI, case study, one-line CTA).
- Day 5: Follow-up 2 (offer quick win, short demo or worksheet).
- Day 8: Follow-up 3 (risk reversal, money-back-like guarantee options).
- Day 12: Follow-up 4 (final urgency: slots or pricing changes).
- Day 14: Break-up or re-frame for longer-term nurture.

3) Message Templates (outreach emails + cold DM + call scripts)

A. Email Template 1 – Initial Outreach
Subject: Quick win for [Company] with automated Excel routines
Body:
Hi [Name],
I help [Industry] teams save hours on [specific pain], often turning complex spreadsheets into reliable dashboards. Our approach delivers measurable ROI in days, not weeks. For example, we recently helped [Similar Customer] cut reporting time by 70% and automated [task].
Would you be open to a 15-minute call to explore potential wins for [Company]?
Best,
John | NorthStar Synergy

B. Email Template 2 – Follow-up
Subject: Re: Quick win for [Company]
Body:
Hi [Name],
Following up on my previous note — if you’re evaluating automation for [pain area], we have a few ready-to-prototype options that map to your current data.
Are you available for a 15-minute chat this week? I can tailor a simple ROI calc for your team.
Cheers,
John

C. Email Template 3 – Case Study / Social Proof
Subject: How [Similar Company] saved [X]% time on reporting
Body:
Hi [Name],
Here’s a quick example: [Case Study summary]. If you’d like, I can show a 60-second walkthrough of a custom ROI model for [Company].
Let me know if you want to see a live demo.
Best,
John

D. Call Script – Qualification
Hi [Name], this is John from NorthStar Synergy. We help [industry] teams eliminate [pain] with automation. Quick question: is [pain point] currently costing you time or money? If yes, I can show a fast ROI calc in 10 minutes.

4) Objection Handling Library (brief)
- We’re already using Gmail/our own tools; we can deliver without disrupting current stack.
- ROI is proven; time-to-value is short (days).
- Budget constraints? We offer 3 tiers and scalable ROI models.
- Not the right time? We can schedule for next quarter and share a micro-demo now.

5) Deliverables & Proposals
- Proposals in 3 tiers per service (Good/Better/Best) with clear ROI framing and a 2-3 page impact summary.
- Setup timeline aligned to client’s project calendars.

6) Tracking & CRM Logging
- Log every outreach touch in leads.jsonl; track quotes in jobs.jsonl with stage and expected close date.
- KPIs: weekly new leads, quotes sent, win rate, average deal size, time-to-close.

7) Risk & Compliance
- Ensure TCPA compliance for SMS outreach; keep opt-outs.
- Only reach out to confirmed business emails/contacts; verify website absence before outreach starts.

8) Continuous Improvement
- Review weekly: top 3 wins/losses; refine templates; test new subject lines.
