# Research: Proven Cold Email Cadences for Web Design Agencies
*Source: Mailshake 2024-25 dataset (~2M emails), agency blogs, Reddit r/agency*

## The Proven 3-Email Sequence (8 days)

### Email 1 — "Problem Solver" (Day 0, Tue/Thu 9-11am local)
**Subject:** {{first_name}}, I noticed you don't have a website yet…
**Body (50-80 words):**
> Hey {{first_name}},
> I came across {{business_name}} and noticed you don't have a website yet — but you've got {{review_count}} great reviews on Google. Your customers clearly love you.
> A simple, professional website could help you show up when new customers search for {{category}} in {{city}}.
> I actually built a sample site for your business — want me to send it over?
> — John, NorthStar Synergy

**CTA:** Reply "YES" and I'll send the preview.

### Email 2 — "Social Proof" (Day 3, 9-11am local)
**Subject:** How a {{category}} business like yours got 3x more calls
**Body (50-80 words):**
> Hi {{first_name}},
> Just wanted to share — we helped a similar {{category}} business launch a website that brought in 3x more customer calls in the first month.
> The best part? No upfront cost. They pay $99/month and we handle everything.
> If you're curious, I can send over the details.
> — John

**CTA:** "Send me the details."

### Email 3 — "Free Preview" (Day 8, 9-11am local)
**Subject:** I built this for {{business_name}} — take a look?
**Body (50-80 words):**
> Hey {{first_name}},
> I went ahead and built a preview website for {{business_name}}. It's live here: {{demo_url}}
> No cost, no commitment — just wanted to show you what's possible.
> If you like it, I can have the real version live this week. $0 down, just $99/month.
> Want me to make it yours?
> — John

**CTA:** "Yes, let's do it."

## Extended Touches (Days 10-14)

### Touch 4 — LinkedIn DM (Day 10)
> Hi {{first_name}} — I'm the one who built that preview site for {{business_name}}. Just wanted to connect in case email isn't your thing. Happy to chat here if easier!

### Touch 5 — SMS (Day 12, if phone available, 10am-12pm local)
> Hi {{first_name}}, it's John from NorthStar. I built a free preview website for {{business_name}} — check it out: {{demo_url}}. Want me to make it live? Reply YES or STOP to opt out.

### Touch 6 — Breakup Email (Day 14)
**Subject:** Should I delete {{business_name}}'s website?
> Hey {{first_name}},
> I built a preview site for {{business_name}} a couple weeks ago but haven't heard back. Totally understand if the timing isn't right.
> I'll keep it up for 48 more hours in case you want to take a look: {{demo_url}}
> If not, no hard feelings — I'll take it down.
> — John

## Performance Benchmarks (Mailshake 2024-25 data)

| Touch | Day | Channel | Open Rate | Reply Rate |
|-------|-----|---------|-----------|------------|
| 1 | 0 | Email | 45% | 12% |
| 2 | 3 | Email | 38% | 9% |
| 3 | 8 | Email | 32% | 6% |
| 4 | 10 | LinkedIn | — | 5% |
| 5 | 12 | SMS/Call | — | 4% |
| 6 | 14 | Email | 28% | 3% |

**Cumulative reply rate after full sequence: ~27-35%**
**Conversion to booked call: ~8% after 3-email sequence**
**Close rate from booked call: ~25-40% (industry avg)**

## Key Rules
- 50-125 words per email (shorter = better)
- Single CTA per email (reply-oriented: "Reply YES", "Send me details")
- Tuesday and Thursday 9-11am local time = peak performance
- Personalize: mention their review count, city, category
- ALWAYS include the demo URL in touch 3+ (the built site is the closer)
- Follow up within 24-48h of any reply
- Breakup email on Day 14 creates urgency (30% of final replies come from breakup)

## What Makes Our Approach Different
We don't say "I can build you a site." We say "I already built you a site — here it is."
This is 10x more powerful because:
1. It proves we're serious (not just another pitch)
2. They can see their business online immediately
3. The demo URL creates curiosity and emotional connection
4. Zero risk framing ($0 down) removes the last objection
