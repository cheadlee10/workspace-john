# Research: Successful Agency Models (Web Design / Local SMBs)

Summary of patterns observed across 2024-2026:
- Pricing models
  - Recurring revenue: monthly retainers for ongoing maintenance, hosting, updates, and SEO, commonly in $29–$299/mo ranges for SMBs, with higher tiers at $999+/mo for full-service subscriptions.
  - Hybrid: base project plus optional ongoing maintenance add-ons; often 3-tier pricing (Good/Better/Best).
  - White-label/affiliate revenue via platforms like GoHighLevel SaaS mode to create recurring revenue streams.
- Service packaging
  - Core: website build (landing sites, small business sites) + hosting + basic SEO.
  - Add-ons: ongoing content updates, security, backups, ongoing optimization, local SEO, Google Business optimization, and conversion-rate optimization.
  - Dashboards / reports as value props for trust and ongoing engagement.
- Lead generation and outreach
  - Local audits and free proposals to land initial projects; follow-ups within 24–72 hours with ROI framing.
  - Multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, cold calling, and SMS) with personalized hooks.
- Tools and platforms
  - GoHighLevel SaaS mode for recurring, CRM and marketing automation.
  - Hosting: WordPress / GoDaddy / SquareSpace / Webflow; CMS choice depending on client needs.
  - CRM: Airtable/Notion/HubSpot; frequent use for pipeline management.
- Growth trajectories
  - From 0 to $10K+/month via a mix of 10–20 recurring clients and 5–10 one-off larger projects.
  - Rapid scaling via leverage (outsourcing, white-labels, or platform-based SaaS) once recurring revenue is stabilized.
- Real-world examples and numbers
  - GoHighLevel pricing models and case studies showing scalable recurring revenue via SaaS-like features.
  - Various agency blogs and Reddit discussions showing ranges of $50–$500/mo for small businesses, with higher tiers for comprehensive packages.

Key takeaways to operationalize
- Build a minimum viable recurring revenue stack (e.g., a 3-tier plan: $49/mo, $199/mo, $499/mo) bundled with a fixed set of deliverables and optional add-ons.
- Invest in a repeatable outreach cadence with ROI framing and social proof (case studies / dashboards).
- Leverage a CRM automation layer to nurture leads and convert to recurring revenue quickly.
- Consider a GoHighLevel SaaS mode or similar if you want to scale recurring revenue without proportionally increasing headcount.
